# Saturn's Rings ![Saturn's Rings](./PIA02241-med.jpg) [ Large size JPG image (60745 bytes)](./PIA02241.jpg) **Caption: ** When seen from the unlit side, the rings of Saturn present a much different appearance from that familiar to telescopic observers. Relatively opaque areas like the B Ring turn black, while lightly populated zones, such as the C Ring and the Cassini Division, prove to to excellent diffuse transmitters of sunlight. The A Ring, whith intermediate opacity, is at an intermediate level of brightness.
