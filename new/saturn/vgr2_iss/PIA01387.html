# Saturn's F Ring ![Saturn's F Ring](./PIA01387-med.jpg) [ Large size JPG image (86449 bytes)](./PIA01387.jpg) [ Full size TIFF image (434723 bytes)](./PIA01387.tif) **Caption: ** Voyager 2 took this high-resolution image of Saturn's F ring Aug. 26 from a distance of 51,500 kilometers (32,000 miles). This closeup view shows that the ring is made up of at least four distinct components. A higher-resolution scan through the F ring, the result of the star [occultation](/glossary.html#occultation) experiment conducted by the spacecraft's photopolarimeter, showed even finer structure in the ring. Voyager 1 showed a braiding effect not evident in this picture. The small black dots are [reseau](/glossary.html#reseau) (reference) marks on the camera. The small bright dash in the middle right of the image is a star trail.
