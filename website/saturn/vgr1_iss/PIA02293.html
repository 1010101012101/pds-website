--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # Saturn's F Ring ![Saturn's F Ring](PIA02293-med.jpg) [Large size JPG image (7394 bytes)](PIA02293.jpg) **Caption:** This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's F Ring was taken through the Clear filter while at a distance of 6.9 million km from Saturn on 8 November 1980. The brightness variations of this tightly-constrained ring shown here indicate that the ring is less uniform in makeup than the larger rings.
