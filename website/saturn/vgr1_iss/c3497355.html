--- layout: base --- # Optical Depth of the Rings ![Optical Depth of the Rings](./c3497355-med.gif) [ Large size GIF image (327865 bytes)](./c3497355.gif) **Caption: ** This Voyager image shows the rings passing in front of the planet. As a result, you can see which rings are opaque [(optically thick)](/glossary.html#optical_depth) and which are transparent (optically thin). Part of the middle B Ring obscures the planet completely, while the planet easily shines through the fainter, inner C Ring.
