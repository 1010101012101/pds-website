--- layout: base layout_style: default title: "Optical Depth of the Rings" --- # Optical Depth of the Rings ![Optical Depth of the Rings](c3497355-med.gif) [Full-Res GIF](c3497355.gif) (327 kB) **Caption:** This Voyager image shows the rings passing in front of the planet. As a result, you can see which rings are opaque (optically thick) and which are transparent (optically thin). Part of the middle B Ring obscures the planet completely, while the planet easily shines through the fainter, inner C Ring.
