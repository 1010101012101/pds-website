--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # Saturn's B Ring ![Saturn's B Ring](PIA02289-med.jpg) [Large size JPG image (5356 bytes)](PIA02289.jpg) **Caption:** This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's B Ring and Cassini Division was taken through the Clear filter from a distance of 12.6 million km on 3 November 1980. The Cassini Division separating the A and B Rings is clearly not an empty region. The Division shows several substantial well-defined ringlets. JPL managed the Voyager Project for NASA's Office of Space Science. **Image Note:** PICNO 1416S1-010 Source: [NASA's Planetary Photojournal: Image No. PIA02289](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/?IDNumber=PIA02289)
