--- layout: base layout_style: default title: "PIA02289: Saturn's B Ring" --- # Saturn's B Ring ![PIA02289: Saturn's B Ring](PIA02289-med.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02289.jpg) (5.565 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02289.tif) (32.95 kB) **Caption:** This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's B Ring and Cassini Division was taken through the Clear filter from a distance of 12.6 million km on 3 November 1980. The Cassini Division separating the A and B Rings is clearly not an empty region. The Division shows several substantial well-defined ringlets. JPL managed the Voyager Project for NASA's Office of Space Science. **Image Note:** PICNO 1416S1-010 Source: [NASA's Planetary Photojournal: Image No. PIA02289](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA02289)
