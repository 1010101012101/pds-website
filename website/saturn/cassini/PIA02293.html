--- layout: base layout_style: default title: PIA02293: Saturn's F-Ring --- # PIA02293: Saturn's F-Ring [ ![Saturn's F-Ring](browse/PIA02293.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA02293_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA02293_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02293.jpg) (7.897 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02293.tif) (19.1 kB) **Caption:** This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's F Ring was taken through the Clear filter while at a distance of 6.9 million km from Saturn on 8 November 1980. The brightness variations of this tightly-constrained ring shown here indicate that the ring is less uniform in makeup than the larger rings.
