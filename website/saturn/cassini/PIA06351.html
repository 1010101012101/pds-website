It was re-projected to appear as a straight line. The bright object to the lower left is the moon Prometheus, which appears elongated due to the re-projection. A faint stream of material, or streamer, appears to be connecting the moon to the ring. This streamer probably formed when Prometheus bobbed up and down in its orbit around Saturn and caused periodic perturbations to the F ring. The vertical scale is from 139, 000 kilometers to 141,000 kilometers (86,300 miles to 87,600 miles). The horizontal scale is 20 degrees, or one-eighteenth of the entire F ring.
