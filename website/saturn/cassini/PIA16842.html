--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # PIA16842: Saturn's Ring 'Rain' (Artist Concept) [ ![ Saturn's Ring 'Rain' (Artist Concept)](browse/PIA16842.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA16842_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA16842_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16842.jpg) (717.3 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16842.tif) (55.15 MB) **Caption:** This artist's concept illustrates how charged water particles flow into the Saturnian atmosphere from the planet's rings, causing a reduction in atmospheric brightness.
