--- layout: base layout_style: default title: "PIA06093: Two Waves in One Spectacular Image of Saturn's Rings" --- # PIA06093: Two Waves in One Spectacular Image of Saturn's Rings [ ![Two Waves in One Spectacular Image of Saturn's Rings](browse/PIA06093.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA06093_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA06093_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06093.jpg) (238.9 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06093.tif) (247.1 kB) **Caption:** This image shows a bending wave (right) and density wave in Saturn's A ring, interior to the Encke Gap. It was taken by the narrow angle camera on the Cassini spacecraft after successful entry into Saturn's orbit. The view shows the dark, or unlit, side of the rings. **Image Credit:** NASA/JPL/Space Science Institute Source: [NASA's Planetary Photojournal: Image No. PIA06093](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA06093)
