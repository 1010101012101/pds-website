This gap marks the inner edge of the Cassini Division, within which the five dim bands at left reside. This view looks toward the sunlit side of the rings from about 6 degrees below the ringplane. The image was taken in visible light with the Cassini spacecraft narrow-angle camera on Sept. 29, 2007. The view was obtained at a distance of approximately 1 million kilometers (637,000 miles) from Saturn. Image scale is 6 kilometers (4 miles) per pixel.
