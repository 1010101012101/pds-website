See [PIA07567](http://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA07567) for a closer view of Epimetheus (116 kilometers, or 72 miles across). The image was taken in visible light with the Cassini spacecraft narrow-angle camera on Dec. 20, 2005, at a distance of approximately 2.3 million kilometers (1.4 million miles) from Epimetheus and 2.2 million kilometers (1.4 million miles) from Saturn. The image scale is 14 kilometers (9 miles) per pixel on Epimetheus and 13 kilometers (8 miles) per pixel on Saturn.
