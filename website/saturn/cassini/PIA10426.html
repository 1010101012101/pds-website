This view looks toward the sunlit side of the rings from about 15 degrees below the ringplane. The image was taken in visible light with the Cassini spacecraft narrow-angle camera on June 17, 2008. The view was obtained at a distance of approximately 937,000 kilometers (582,000 miles) from Saturn. Image scale is 5 kilometers (3 miles) per pixel.
