--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # PIA06511: Shadow of the Rings [ ![Shadow of the Rings](browse/PIA06511.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA06511_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA06511_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06511.jpg) (24.15 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06511.tif) (578 kB) **Caption:** The inner edges of Saturn's rings arc gracefully across the top of this image from Cassini. Thin shadows cast by the rings are visible at upper right through the optically thin C ring.
