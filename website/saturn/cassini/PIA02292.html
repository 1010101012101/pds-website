--- layout: base layout_style: default title: PIA02292: Saturn's F-Ring --- # PIA02292: Saturn's F-Ring [ ![Saturn's F-Ring](browse/PIA02292.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA02292_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA02292_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02292.jpg) (58.77 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02292.tif) (115 kB) **Caption:** This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's F Ring was taken through the Clear filter while at a distance of 0.75 million km from Saturn on 12 November 1980. The kinks and braids of this tightly-constrained ring are visible along with the outer edge of the A Ring.
