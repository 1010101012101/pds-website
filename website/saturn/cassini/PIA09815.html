The view was taken from about 23 degrees above the ringplane and looks toward the unilluminated side of the rings. The image was taken with the Cassini spacecraft wide-angle camera on Dec. 17, 2007 using a spectral filter sensitive to wavelengths of infrared light centered at 752 nanometers. The view was obtained at a distance of approximately 1.3 million kilometers (791,000 miles) from Saturn. Image scale is 72 kilometers (45 miles) per pixel. The Cassini-Huygens mission is a cooperative project of NASA, the European Space Agency and the Italian Space Agency.
