--- layout: base layout_style: default title: PIA06092: Cassini Captures the Cassini Division --- # PIA06092: Cassini Captures the Cassini Division [ ![Cassini Captures the Cassini Division](browse/PIA06092.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA06092_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA06092_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06092.jpg) (200.8 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06092.tif) (438.5 kB) **Caption:** This image shows the region of Saturn's rings known as the Cassini Division. It was taken by the narrow angle camera on the Cassini spacecraft after successful entry into Saturn's orbit. The view shows the dark, or unlit, side of the rings. **Image Credit:** NASA/JPL/Space Science Institute Source: [NASA's Planetary Photojournal: Image No. PIA06092](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA06092)
