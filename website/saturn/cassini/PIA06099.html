--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # PIA06099: The Encke Gap as Never Seen Before [ ![The Encke Gap as Never Seen Before](browse/PIA06099.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA06099_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA06099_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06099.jpg) (43.2 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06099.tif) (750.9 kB) **Caption:** This image shows in superb detail the region in Saturn's rings known as the Encke Gap. It was taken by the narrow angle camera on the Cassini spacecraft after successful entry into Saturn's orbit.
