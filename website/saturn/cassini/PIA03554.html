--- layout: base layout_style: default title: PIA03554: Enceladus Animation --- # PIA03554: Enceladus Animation [ ![ Enceladus Animation](browse/PIA03554.jpg)](jpegMod/PIA03554_modest.jpg) [Medium-Res JPEG](jpegMod/PIA03554_modest.jpg) [Full-Res JPEG](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03554.jpg) (6.463 kB) [Full-Res TIFF](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03554.tif) (228.5 kB) [QuickTime movie](//photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/archive/PIA03554.mov) (8.964 MB) **Caption:** This animation shows the Cassini spacecraft approaching Saturn's icy moon Enceladus. It shows the highest resolution images obtained of the moon's surface. This is followed by a depiction of Saturn's magnetic field, which interacts with Enceladus' atmosphere and presumed plume coming from the south pole.
