It is noteworthy that such clouds are visible here in the shadows cast by the rings. Prometheus is 102 kilometers (63 miles) across. The image was taken in visible light with the Cassini spacecraft narrow-angle camera on June 3, 2005, at a distance of approximately 2.1 million kilometers (1.3 million miles) from Saturn. The image scale is 13 kilometers (8 miles) per pixel. This view was processed to enhance fine details.
