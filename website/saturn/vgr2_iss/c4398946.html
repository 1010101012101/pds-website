--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # Closeup of the Cassini Division ![Closeup of the Cassini Division](./c4398946-med.gif) [ Large size GIF image (276992 bytes)](./c4398946.gif) **Caption:** The Cassini Division, separating Saturn's A and B Rings, is maintained by the 2:1 [resonance](/glossary.html#resonance) with Mimas. It was once thought to be empty, but this closeup view, Voyager 2 image [FDS](/glossary.html#fds) 43989.46, shows that it really contains several broad belts of material separated by empty "rifts".
