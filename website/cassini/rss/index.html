### Update September 2018: An updated Cassini RSS Data User Guide (Version 1.1) is available. * RSS Data User Guide PDF ### Update May 2014: RSS Radio Occultation Profiles * The PDS Rings node has posted a dataset of Saturn ring radial profiles derived from RSS radio occultation data. The data include ring radial profiles at 1 and 10 km resolution from more than 30 high quality RSS occultations. The dataset has completed peer review and lien resolution. * Users may browse the dataset [CORSS_8001](/link/volumes/CORSS_8xxx_lien_resolution/CORSS_8001/), or download the entire dataset [CORSS_8001 tar.gz](/link/archives-volumes/CORSS_8xxx_lien_resolution/CORSS_8001.tar.gz). NOTE: Raw RSS data may be obtained from the [PDS Atmospheres Node](//pds- atmospheres.nmsu.edu/).
