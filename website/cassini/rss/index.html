### Update September 2018: An updated Cassini RSS Data User Guide (Version 1.1) is available. * RSS Data User Guide PDF ### RSS Radio Occultation Profiles * The PDS Rings node has posted a dataset of Saturn ring radial profiles derived from RSS radio occultation data obtained during the prime mission. The data include ring radial profiles at 1 and 10 km resolution from more than 30 high quality RSS occultations. * You may browse the dataset [CORSS_8001](/viewmaster/volumes/CORSS_8xxx), or download the entire dataset [CORSS_8001 tar.gz](/viewmaster/archives-volumes/CORSS_8xxx/). * Version 2 of this dataset, which includes the addition of highest practical resolution, pre-diffraction corrected data, and expands the dataset coverage to the entire mission, is available by request and will be posted online in early 2019. In the mean time, you may request the data using the Questions/Feedback button to the left. NOTE: Raw RSS data may be obtained from the [PDS Atmospheres Node](//pds- atmospheres.nmsu.edu/).
