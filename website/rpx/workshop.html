--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # Tucson Saturn Ring Plane Crossing Workshop May 26-27, 1994 A workshop has been held to discuss observing opportunities for the 1995/96 passages of the Earth and Sun through Saturn's ring plane. The meeting took place Thursday and Friday, May 26-27, 1994 at the University of Arizona in Tucson. It was sponsored jointly by the Planetary Data System Rings Node and the Lunar and Planetary Laboratory of the University of Arizona.
