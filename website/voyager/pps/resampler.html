--- layout: base layout_style: default tabs: voyager_pps --- ## PPS Resampler 1.0 This tool generates custom ring profiles at uniformly-spaced radial samples. You can generate files using different calibration and geometry models, different radial sampling, and with different columns than the [standard set](profiles.html). Just fill in the form with your desired parameters and click on the "Generate Table" button below. Click [here](resampler_help.html) for help, or click on individual highlighted items. ## Occultation Options Select the planet and then choose the occultation parameters in that column.
