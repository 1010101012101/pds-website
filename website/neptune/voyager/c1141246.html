--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # Twisted Rope ![Twisted Rope](c1141246-med.gif) [ Large size GIF image (238200 bytes)](c1141246.gif) **Caption:** A closeup of the Fraternity arc in Voyager 2 image [FDS](/glossary.html#fds) 11412.46 looks like a twisted rope. The rope "strands" are really individual clumps that have smeared out during the 111 second exposure. The clumps are only 0.5 to 0.8 degrees apart and are very difficult to understand.
