--- layout: base layout_style: default title: "C1141246: Twisted Rope" --- # Twisted Rope ![Twisted Rope](c1141246-med.gif) [Full-Res GIF](c1141246.gif) (238 kB) **Caption:** A closeup of the Fraternity arc in Voyager 2 image [FDS](/glossary.html#fds) 11412.46 looks like a twisted rope. The rope "strands" are really individual clumps that have smeared out during the 111 second exposure. The clumps are only 0.5 to 0.8 degrees apart and are very difficult to understand.
