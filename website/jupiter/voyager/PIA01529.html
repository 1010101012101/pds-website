--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # Jupiter Ring System ![Jupiter Ring System](./PIA01529-med.jpg) [ Large size JPG image (52955 bytes)](./PIA01529.jpg) [ Full size TIFF image (363806 bytes)](./PIA01529.tif) **Caption: ** Jupiter's faint ring system is shown in this color composite as two light orange lines protruding from the left toward Jupiter's limb. This picture was taken in Jupiter's shadow through orange and violet filters. The colorful images of Jupiter's bright limb are evidence of the spacecraft motion during these long exposures. The Voyager 2 spacecraft was at a range of 1,450,000 kilometers (900,000 miles) about two degrees below the plane of the ring. The lower ring image was cut short by Jupiter's shadow on the ring. JPL manages the Voyager project for NASA's Office of Space Science. **Image Note:** This image was scanned from physical media.
