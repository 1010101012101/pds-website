--- layout: base layout_style: default --- # The "Best" Voyager Jovian Ring Images Although there are other images labeled as "J_RINGS" on various Voyager CDROMs, the general consensus is that the following 25 images represent the "best" (scientifically useful) images available from the Voyager 1 and 2 spacecraft. They sample a limited range of viewing geometries with about half taken in backscattered light (i.e. low solar phase angles) and the other half in forward scatter, during the brief interval as Voyager 2 passed through Jupiter's shadow. Several of the frames were taken through color filters, allowing limited spectral coverage [ (Showalter et al. 1987)](/reference/abstracts/showalter1987_01.html).
