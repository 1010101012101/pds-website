Links to ROSES support pages at the other PDS Nodes can be found at the PDS Engineering Node's [ ROSES support](http://pds.nasa.gov/ROSES/roses.shtml) page. ### Notes ![\[SPACER\]](css/spacer.gif) ** Each ROSES NRA discussed here includes specific requirements concerning the public availability and quality of data intended for use in proposed research. For most of these programs, the criteria for data available through the PDS include whether or not the data has been certified by PDS. On the "Data Status" tab, we identify the availability date and certified status of each of the data sets or data volumes in the Rings Node holdings. ** Since several of the programs include encouragement or requirements to archive the results of the proposed research, the "Archiving" tab provides information and links to assist with archive planning and incorporating archive planning in proposals. The other tabs associated with this page provide information on data at the the Rings Node relevant to the specific NRA identified in the tab name, and links to holdings at other PDS nodes which also may be relevant. The following table gives the key dates for each of the NRA's supported by these pages based on ROSES 2012. We will attempt to keep these pages current as relevant amendments to ROSES 2012 are released. However, the definitive source is of course the [NSPIRES web site.](http://nspires.nasaprs.com/external/) **Latest update:** 8/22/12 based on Amendment 13 changing the OPR due date. ### The Rings Related Research Programs ![\[SPACER\]](css/spacer.gif) The links in the following table are to the respective Rings Node support pages (same destinations as the tabs at the top of the page).
