--- layout: base layout_style: default tabs: roses_2013 --- ## Status and Availability dates ### Introduction The following table lists data held at the PDS Rings Node, generally by Volume or Dataset ID, the date the data became publicly available, it's PDS certification status, and the date the volume was certified. * **Dates** In order to reduce the size of the table, for large, imcrementally delivered data sets (e.g., Cassini data volumes), **all data available on or before January 1st, 2012 are show as available on 2012-01-01**. For example, this allows us to group all 67 volumes of Cassini ISS Saturn data submitted between Jul 1st, 2005 and January 1st, 2012 on a single line. Certification date is handled in the same fashion. * **PDS Certification** Data sets that are appropriate for inclusion in NASA data analysis and other proposals are those that have successfully completed the PDS peer review process, or are about to pass, pending resolution of minor liens that the review panel and relevant PDS Node deem not to impact the scientific integrity of the data set. These data sets are considered "certified" by the PDS. Data sets (or newly delivered, non-reviewed increments to dynamic data sets within archives) that do not meet the above criteria are considered "non-certified" and are clearly labeled as such on PDS web sites.
