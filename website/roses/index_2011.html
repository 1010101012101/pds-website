Links to ROSES support pages at the other PDS Nodes can be found at the PDS Engineering Node's [ ROSES support](http://pds.nasa.gov/ROSES/roses.shtml) page. The full ROSES document can be accessed under **Solicitations** at the [NSPIRES web site.](http://nspires.nasaprs.com/external/) ![\[SPACER\]](css/spacer.gif) ### The Rings Related Research Programs ![\[SPACER\]](css/spacer.gif) ROSES NRA's include specific requirements concerning the public availability and quality of data intended for use in proposed research. To assist proposers, we identify the availability date and status of each of the data sets or data volumes in the Rings Node holdings on the "Data Status" tab. Since several of the programs include encouragement or requirements to archive the results of the proposed research, the "Archiving" tab provides information and links to assist with archive planning and incorporating archive planning in proposals. The other tabs associated with this page provide information on data at the the Rings Node relevant to the specific NRA identified in the tab name, and links to holdings at other PDS nodes which also may be relevant. The following table gives the key dates for each of the NRA's supported by these pages based on the initial release of ROSES 2011, February 18, 2011. We will attempt to keep these pages current as relevant amendments to ROSES 2011 are released. However, the definitive source is of course the [NSPIRES web site.](http://nspires.nasaprs.com/external/) The links in the following table are to the respective Rings Node support pages (same destinations as the tabs at the top of the page).
