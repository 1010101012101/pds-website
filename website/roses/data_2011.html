For example, this allows us to group all 67 volumes of Cassini ISS Saturn data submitted between Jul 1st, 2005 and January 1st, 2012 on a single line. Certification date is handled in the same fashion. * **PDS Certification** Data sets that are appropriate for inclusion in NASA data analysis and other proposals are those that have successfully completed the PDS peer review process, or are about to pass, pending resolution of minor liens that the review panel and relevant PDS Node deem not to impact the scientific integrity of the data set. These data sets are considered "certified" by the PDS. Data sets (or newly delivered, non-reviewed increments to dynamic data sets within archives) that do not meet the above criteria are considered "non-certified" and are clearly labeled as such on PDS web sites. ![\[SPACER\]](css/spacer.gif) Data Set or Volume ID | Date Available | Certification Status | Date Certified ---|---|---|--- Astrometry_0001 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 Astrometry_0101 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COCIRS_0010 through COCIRS_1103 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COCIRS_5401 through COCIRS_6006 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COISS_0001 through COISS_0011 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COISS_1001 through COISS_1009 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COISS_2001 through COISS_2067 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COISS_3001 through COISS_3007 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COUVIS_0001 through COUVIS_0034 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COUVIS_0001 through COVIMS_0046 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 EBROCC_0001 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 GO_0017 through GO_0023 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 NHJULO_1001 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 NHJULO_2001 through NHJULO_2003 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 NHSP_1000 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 RPX_0001 through RPX_0005 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 RPX_0101 through RPX_0401 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 VGISS_0004,VGISS_0005, | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 VGISS_0026 through VGISS_0038 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 VG_0001 through VG_0038 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 VG_2001 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 VG_2801, VG_2802, VG_2810 | 2012-01-01 | certified | 2012-01-01 COUVIS_8001 | 2012-01-01 | non-certified | -
