# Texture of Rock at 'Jibsheet' * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07979_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07979.jpg) (934 x 701) (104.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07979.tif) (934 x 701) (655.7 kB)

Caption:

A bulbous texture is evident in this rock target at the outcrop called "Jibsheet" in this view from the microscopic imager on NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit. Frames making up this mosaic image of a target dubbed "Reef" were taken during the rover's 481st martian day, or sol (May 11, 2005).
