# Meridiani Valleys * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07454_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07454.jpg) (640 x 1550) (173.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07454.tif) (640 x 1550) (993.3 kB)

Caption:

10 March 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows layered sedimentary rocks and the traces of valleys that were once underneath those rocks in northwestern Sinus Meridiani.

Location near : 4.5°N, 2.4°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Northern Summer
