# North Polar Ice * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07329_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07329.jpg) (1177 x 1536) (653.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07329.tif) (1177 x 1536) (1.8 MB)

Caption:

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image illustrates very well the detailed, pitted nature of the north polar residual ice cap. As water ice sublimes away a little bit each summer, dark-floored pits have formed, trapping dust and other debris.
