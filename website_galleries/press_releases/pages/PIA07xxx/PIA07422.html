# Features in Argyre * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07422_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07422.jpg) (512 x 813) (60.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07422.tif) (512 x 813) (352.5 kB)

Caption:

28 February 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows landforms, including large, windblown ripples, on the floor of the ancient, giant Argyre impact basin.

Location near : 48.0°S, 42.2°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Winter
