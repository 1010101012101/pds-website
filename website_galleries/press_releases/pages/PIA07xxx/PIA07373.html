# Crater in Acidalia * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07373_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07373.jpg) (512 x 765) (101.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07373.tif) (512 x 765) (392.3 kB)

Caption:

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows an impact crater and associated bright wind streak in Acidalia Planitia. Dozens of smaller craters dot the scene.

Location near : 33.6°N, 25.0°W
Image width : ~3.0 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Summer
