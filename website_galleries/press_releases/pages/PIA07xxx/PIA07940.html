# Athabasca Streamlines * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07940_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07940.jpg) (672 x 2000) (233.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07940.tif) (672 x 2000) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

11 May 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows streamlined landforms carved by catastrophic floods in the Athabasca Valles system of the Cerberus region of Mars.

Location near : 7.9°N, 205.7°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Autumn
