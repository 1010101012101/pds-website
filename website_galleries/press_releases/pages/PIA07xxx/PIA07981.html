# 'Larry's Outcrop' in False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07981_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07981.jpg) (350 x 350) (34.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07981.tif) (350 x 350) (368.1 kB)

Caption:

A portion of an exposure of bedrock dubbed "Larry's Outcrop" shows little layering in this view, in contrast to nearby outcrops called "Methuselah" and "Jibsheet." NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit used its panoramic camera in May 2005 to take this image, which is presented in false color.
