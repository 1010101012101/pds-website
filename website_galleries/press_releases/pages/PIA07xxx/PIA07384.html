# Hecates Tholus Channels * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07384_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07384.jpg) (512 x 2527) (249.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07384.tif) (512 x 2527) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

24 February 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows narrow channels and small impact craters on the north flank of the Elysium volcano, Hecates Tholus.

Location near : 34.0°N, 210.3°W
Image width : ~1 km (~0.6 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Winter
