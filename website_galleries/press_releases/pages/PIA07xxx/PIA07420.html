# Ripples and Rocks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07420_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07420.jpg) (549 x 777) (91.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07420.tif) (549 x 777) (427.3 kB)

Caption:

26 February 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows light-toned sedimentary rock outcrops and large dark-toned, windblown ripples in Aram Chaos.

Location near : 3.0°N, 20.9°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Northern Summer
