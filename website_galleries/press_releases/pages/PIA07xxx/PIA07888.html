# Trough in Tempe * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07888_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07888.jpg) (672 x 930) (91.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07888.tif) (672 x 930) (625.8 kB)

Caption:

28 April 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a trough partly filled by large, windblown ripples in the Tempe Terra region of Mars.

Location near : 36.5°N, 65.3°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Summer
