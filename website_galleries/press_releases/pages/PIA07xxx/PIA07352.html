# Sedimentary Rock in Candor * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07352_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07352.jpg) (627 x 1133) (173.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07352.tif) (627 x 1133) (711.3 kB)

Caption:

11 February 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows dozens of light- and a few dark-toned sedimentary rock layers exposed by faulting and erosion in western Candor Chasma, part of the vast Valles Marineris trough system.

Location near : 6.5°S, 77.0°W
Image width : ~~3.0 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Autumn
