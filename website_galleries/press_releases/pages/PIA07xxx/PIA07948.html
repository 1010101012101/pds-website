# Inverted Channels * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07948_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07948.jpg) (672 x 1144) (201.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07948.tif) (672 x 1144) (769.8 kB)

Caption:

15 May 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows the inverted traces of old channels in a complex, wind-eroded fan located in the Aeolis region of Mars.

Location near : 6.3°S, 208.9°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Winter
