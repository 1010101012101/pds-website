# Polar Polygons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07354_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07354.jpg) (1024 x 1559) (419.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07354.tif) (1024 x 1559) (1.6 MB)

Caption:

13 February 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows polygons formed in ice-rich material in the north polar region of Mars. The bright surfaces in this image are covered by a thin water ice frost.

Location near : 79.8°N, 344.8°W
Image width : ~1.5 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Summer
