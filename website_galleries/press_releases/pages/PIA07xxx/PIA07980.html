# Spirit View of 'Larry's Outcrop' * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07980_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07980.jpg) (467 x 350) (24.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07980.tif) (467 x 350) (163.8 kB)

Caption:

An exposure of bedrock dubbed "Larry's Outcrop" on the flank of "Husband Hill" inside Mars' Gusev Crater shows little layering in this view. NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit used its panoramic camera in May 2005 to take the images combined into this mosaic.
