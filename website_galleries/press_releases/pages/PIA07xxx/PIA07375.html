# December's Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07375_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07375.jpg) (836 x 2134) (299.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07375.tif) (836 x 2134) (1.7 MB)

Caption:

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows sand dunes in the north polar region of Mars, as they appeared during northern summer in December 2004.

Location near : 78.1°N, 227.2°W
Image width : ~3.0 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Summer
