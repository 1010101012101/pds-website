# Heat Shield's Main Piece * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07188_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07188.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (70.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07188.tif) (1024 x 1024) (343.6 kB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity caught this view of the main piece of the spacecraft's heat shield during the rover's 328th martian day, or sol (Dec. 25, 2004). A separation spring can be seen on the ground to the lower left side of the heat shield.
