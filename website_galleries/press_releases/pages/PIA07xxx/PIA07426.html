# Bright Devil Streaks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07426_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07426.jpg) (672 x 2983) (250.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07426.tif) (672 x 2983) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

4 March 2005
In honor of Giovanni V. Schiaparelli's 170th birthday, we present this Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image showing light-toned dust devil streaks on the southern floor of Schiaparelli Crater.

Location near : 5.3°S, 343.3°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Autumn
