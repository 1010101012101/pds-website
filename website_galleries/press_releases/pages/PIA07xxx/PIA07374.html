# Sedimentary Rocks in Melas * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07374_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07374.jpg) (672 x 948) (84.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07374.tif) (672 x 948) (538.8 kB)

Caption:

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a butte and several other landforms eroded into light-toned, layered, sedimentary rock in southern Melas Chasma. Melas is part of the vast Valles Marineris trough system.

Location near : 11.8°S, 74.6°W
Image width : ~3.0 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Southern Spring
