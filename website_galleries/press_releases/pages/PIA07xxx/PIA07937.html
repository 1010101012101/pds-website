# Russell Dune Gullies * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07937_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07937.jpg) (1280 x 1696) (294.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07937.tif) (1280 x 1696) (2.2 MB)

Caption:

8 May 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows gullies on a large slip face in the Russell Crater dune field. When the image was acquired, the dunes were still covered with seasonal frost.

Location near : 54.7°S, 347.0°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Winter
