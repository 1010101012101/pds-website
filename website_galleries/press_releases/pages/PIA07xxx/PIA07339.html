# Syrian Dust Devil * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07339_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07339.jpg) (512 x 768) (128.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07339.tif) (512 x 768) (393.8 kB)

Caption:

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a dust devil in far western Syria Planum. The dust devil is located near the left-center of the image. It is casting a shadow toward the lower right (southeast).

Location near : 14.5°S, 109.6°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Winter
