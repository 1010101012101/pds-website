# 'Jibsheet' in False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA07xxx/PIA07975_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA07975.jpg) (467 x 350) (30.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA07975.tif) (467 x 350) (491.1 kB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit drove up to this outcrop, called "Jibsheet," on the flank of "Husband Hill," in early March 2005. This view of Jibsheet by Spirit's panoramic camera is presented in false color.
