# Mars in Stereo * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05008_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05008.jpg) (863 x 428) (1.1 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05008.tif) (863 x 428) (20.2 MB)

Caption:

This image shows the martian terrain in 3-D. The Mars Exploration Rover Spirit captured the image with its two high-resolution stereo panoramic cameras.
