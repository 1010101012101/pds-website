# Meridiani Planum Soil * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05140_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05140.jpg) (512 x 512) (40.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05140.tif) (512 x 512) (262.6 kB)

Caption:

This image shows one of the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity's first views of the martian soil after its successful landing at Meridiani Planum on Mars. Opportunity landed Saturday night at approximately 9:05 PST. The image was taken by the rover's panoramic camera.
