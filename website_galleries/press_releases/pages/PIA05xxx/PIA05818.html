# Spirit's View on Sol 110 (polar) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05818_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05818.jpg) (3981 x 3981) (1.1 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05818.tif) (3981 x 3981) (8.3 MB)

Caption:

This polar projection was assembled from images taken by the navigation camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit on sol 110 (April 24, 2004) at a region dubbed "site 35." Spirit is sitting approximately 33 meters (100 feet) away from the northeast rim of "Missoula" crater.
