# Shrouded in Dust * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05005_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05005.jpg) (3322 x 2934) (50.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05005.tif) (3322 x 2934) (949.9 kB)

Caption:

Dust-covered rocks can be seen in this portion of the 3-D image taken by the panoramic camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit. Scientists plan to use the rover's rock abrasion tool to grind away dusty and weathered rock, exposing fresh rock underneath.
