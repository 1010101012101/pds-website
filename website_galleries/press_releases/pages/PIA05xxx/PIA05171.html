# Engineers Test Roll-Off at JPL * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05171_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05171.jpg) (1920 x 1080) (356.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05171.tif) (1920 x 1080) (6.2 MB)

Caption:

This image taken at JPL shows engineers testing the route by which the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity will roll off its lander. Opportunity touched down at Meridiani Planum, Mars on Jan. 24, 9:05 p.m. PST, 2004, Earth-received time.
