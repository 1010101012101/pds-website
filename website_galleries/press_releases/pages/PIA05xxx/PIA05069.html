# Ready to Roll * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05069_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05069.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (105.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05069.tif) (1024 x 1024) (906.7 kB)

Caption:

This image shows the view from the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit after it successfully completed a 115 degree turn to face northwest, the direction it will roll off the lander. The image was taken by the rover's front hazard avoidance camera.
