# Panoramic View of Lander During Turn * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05083_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05083.jpg) (2867 x 1001) (250.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05083.tif) (2867 x 1001) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

This 360-degree panoramic mosaic image composed of data from the hazard avoidance camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit shows a view of the lander from under the rover deck. The images were taken as the rover turned from its landing position 95 degrees toward the northwest side of the lander.
