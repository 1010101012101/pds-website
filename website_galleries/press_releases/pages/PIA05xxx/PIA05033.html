# Spirit Rises at JPL * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05033_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05033.jpg) (1920 x 1080) (219.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05033.tif) (1920 x 1080) (6.2 MB)

Caption:

This image taken at JPL shows engineers testing the rover stand-up motions. The rover is in an elevated pose, preparing to fold down its wheels, descend and stand up.
