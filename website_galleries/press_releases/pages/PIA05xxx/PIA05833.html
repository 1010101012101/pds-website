# Opportunity Spies Its Target * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05833_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05833.jpg) (2356 x 1066) (284.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05833.tif) (2356 x 1066) (2.2 MB)

Caption:

This is a forward-looking view of the Meridiani Planum plains that lie between the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity and its primary drive target, "Endurance Crater." The images in this image mosaic were taken by the rover's panoramic camera on sol 88.
