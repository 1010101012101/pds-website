# Ready to Roll-2 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05080_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05080.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (146.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05080.tif) (1024 x 1024) (1.0 MB)

Caption:

This image shows the view from the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit after it successfully completed a 115 degree turn to face northwest, the direction it will roll off the lander. The image was taken by the rover's navigation camera.
