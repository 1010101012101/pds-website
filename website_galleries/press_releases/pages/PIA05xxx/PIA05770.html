# Spirit's View on Sol 100 (cylindrical) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05770_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05770.jpg) (7466 x 968) (965.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05770.tif) (7466 x 968) (6.0 MB)

Caption:

This cylindrical-projection mosaic was created from navigation camera images that NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit acquired on sol 100 (April 14, 2004). It reveals Spirit's view after a century of sols on the martian surface.
