# Martian Soil in 3-D * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05095_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05095.jpg) (970 x 843) (208.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05095.tif) (970 x 843) (2.1 MB)

Caption:

This image taken by the microscopic imager on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit shows the powdery soil of Mars in 3-D. It is the sharpest look yet at the surface of another planet. The microscopic imager is located on the rover's instrument deployment device, or "arm."
