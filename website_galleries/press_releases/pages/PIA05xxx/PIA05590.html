# Ma'adim Vallis From the Top * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05590_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05590.jpg) (1920 x 1080) (85.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05590.tif) (1920 x 1080) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

This is a still from an animation showing the geography of Ma'adim Vallis, a valley or channel that enters Gusev Crater. The view of the crater is from the northwest, which is not the direction from which Spirit approached the crater as it landed.
