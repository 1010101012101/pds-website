# Opportunity Digs * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05309_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05309.jpg) (692 x 609) (31.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05309.tif) (692 x 609) (340.0 kB)

Caption:

This image is a screenshot from a computer-generated animation showing the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity trenching a hole in the sandy soil at Meridiani Planum, Mars. Data taken during trenching by the rover's onboard sensors were used to create the movie.
