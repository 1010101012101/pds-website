# Spirit's View on Sol 110 (vertical) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05819_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05819.jpg) (4000 x 4000) (1.5 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05819.tif) (4000 x 4000) (12.3 MB)

Caption:

This vertical projection was assembled from images taken by the navigation camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit on sol 110 (April 24, 2004) at a region dubbed "site 35." Spirit is sitting approximately 33 meters (100 feet) away from the northeast rim of "Missoula" crater.
