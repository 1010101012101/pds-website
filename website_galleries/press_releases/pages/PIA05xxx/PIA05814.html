# Spirit's View on Sol 110 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05814_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05814.jpg) (7466 x 957) (841.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05814.tif) (7466 x 957) (5.6 MB)

Caption:

This cylindrical-projection mosaic was assembled from images taken by the navigation camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit on sol 110 (April 24, 2004) at a region dubbed "site 35." Spirit is sitting approximately 33 meters (100 feet) away from the northeast rim of "Missoula" crater.
