# Spirit's View on Sol 101 (cylindrical) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05776_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05776.jpg) (7466 x 965) (728.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05776.tif) (7466 x 965) (5.3 MB)

Caption:

This cylindrical-projection mosaic was created from navigation camera images that NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit acquired on sol 101 (April 15, 2004). It reveals Spirit's view just before a stopping-point dubbed "Missoula Crater." The rover is on its way to the "Columbia Hills."
