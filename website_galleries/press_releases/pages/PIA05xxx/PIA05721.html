# Looking Back, Spirit Sol 90 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05721_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05721.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (192.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05721.tif) (1024 x 1024) (1.0 MB)

Caption:

Following a long drive, NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit took this backwards glance at its tracks across the landscape on sol 90 of its mission (April 4, 2004). The image is from Spirit's navigation camera.
