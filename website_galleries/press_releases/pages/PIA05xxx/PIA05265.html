# Comparison of a Computer Graphic Model of the Opportunity Lander and Rover with MOC Orbital Image * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05265_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05265.jpg) (6725 x 4500) (825.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05265.tif) (6725 x 4500) (12.7 MB)

Caption:

A computer graphics model of the Opportunity lander and rover are super-imposed on top of the martian terrain where Opportunity landed. This image is a side-by-side combination of PIA05230 and PIA05231 .
