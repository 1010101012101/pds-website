# One Year Before Mission Success * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05840_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05840.jpg) (535 x 355) (48.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05840.tif) (535 x 355) (570.6 kB)

Caption:

This scene from NASA Kennedy Space Center in April 2003 shows work during final processing of the spacecraft then known as Mars Exploration Rover B and later named Opportunity. At this point, the rover was fully assembled for flight and being prepared for final integration with its lander.
