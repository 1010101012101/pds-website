# Opportunity View on Sol 109 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05964_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05964.jpg) (2663 x 977) (382.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05964.tif) (2663 x 977) (2.2 MB)

Caption:

This cylindrical-projection mosaic was created from three navigation camera frames that NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity acquired on sol 109, May 15, 2004. Opportunity is sitting along the rim of "Endurance Crater" in the Meridiani Planum region.
