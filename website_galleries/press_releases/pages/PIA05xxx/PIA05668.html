# On a Roll * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05668_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05668.jpg) (1024 x 768) (86.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05668.tif) (1024 x 768) (668.4 kB)

Caption:

This image from the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity front left hazard-avoidance camera shows a pebble caught in rover's front right wheel. The image from sol 63 of the mission was taken as the rover approached the rock dubbed "Bounce" for analysis.
