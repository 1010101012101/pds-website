# Microscope on Mars * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05195_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05195.jpg) (512 x 512) (31.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05195.tif) (512 x 512) (232.6 kB)

Caption:

This image taken at Meridiani Planum, Mars by the panoramic camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity shows the rover's microscopic imager (circular device in center), located on its instrument deployment device, or "arm." The image was acquired on the ninth martian day or sol of the rover's mission.
