# Over the Rover * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05151_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05151.jpg) (1705 x 1702) (245.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05151.tif) (1705 x 1702) (1.9 MB)

Caption:

This image from the navigation camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity has been projected to show an overhead perspective of the rover. This image was taken shortly after the rover touched down at Meridiani Planum, Mars, at approximately 9:05 p.m. PST on Saturday, Jan. 24.
