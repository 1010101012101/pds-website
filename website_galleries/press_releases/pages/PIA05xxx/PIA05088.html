# Continuing the Turn-2 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05088_med.gif) for a larger view * [Movie Download Options](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/animation/PIA05088)

Caption:

This animation shows the view from the rear hazard avoidance cameras on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit as the rover turns 95 degrees clockwise. This maneuver shows the first and second steps in a 3-point turn that will rotate the rover 115 degrees to face west (the first step previously shown in PIA05052 ) . The rover must make this turn before rolling off the lander because airbags are blocking it from exiting from the front lander petal. Before starting this maneuver, engineers instructed the rover to cut the final cord linking it to the lander.
