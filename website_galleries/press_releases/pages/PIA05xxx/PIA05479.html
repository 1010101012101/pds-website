# Focus on Guadalupe * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05479_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05479.jpg) (1628 x 1272) (293.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05479.tif) (1628 x 1272) (2.0 MB)

Caption:

This mosaic image, taken by the microscopic imager on the Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity, shows a portion of the rock outcrop at Meridiani Planum, Mars, dubbed "Guadalupe." Several images, each showing a different part of "Guadalupe" in good focus, were merged to produce this view.
