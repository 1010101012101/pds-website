# On Its Own * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05137_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05137.jpg) (995 x 761) (84.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05137.tif) (995 x 761) (1.5 MB)

Caption:

This 3-D image combines computer-generated models of the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit and its lander with real surface data from the rover's panoramic camera. It shows Spirit's position just after it rolled off the lander on Jan. 15, 2004.
