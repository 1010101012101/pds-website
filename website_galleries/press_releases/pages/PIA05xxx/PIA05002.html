# Airbag Deflates on Mars * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA05xxx/PIA05002_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA05002.jpg) (541 x 546) (42.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA05002.tif) (541 x 546) (295.9 kB)

Caption:

This image, taken by the navigation camera onboard the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit, shows the airbags used to protect the rover during landing. One bright, dust-covered bag is slightly puffed up against the lander.
