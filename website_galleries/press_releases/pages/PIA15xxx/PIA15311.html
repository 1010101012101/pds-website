# Gale Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15311_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15311.jpg) (729 x 1556) (95.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15311.tif) (729 x 1556) (1.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Gale Crater contains a large layered deposit, part of which is shown in today's VIS image. This deposit is the target of the Curiosity Lander due to land in early August 2012.

Orbit Number: 44524 Latitude: -4.64012 Longitude: 137.685 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-12-28 07:29
