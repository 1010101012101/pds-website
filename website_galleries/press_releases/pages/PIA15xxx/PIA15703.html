# Pavonis Mons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15703_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15703.jpg) (1286 x 2765) (216.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15703.tif) (1286 x 2765) (3.6 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows part of the southern flank of Pavonis Mons. Visible at the bottom of the image are collapse features and lava channels.

Orbit Number: 45493 Latitude: -0.184838 Longitude: 246.512 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2012-03-17 03:39
