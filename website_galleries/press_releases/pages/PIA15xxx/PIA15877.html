# NASA's Vehicle System Test Bed (VSTB) Rover * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15877_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15877.jpg) (7224 x 4821) (2.3 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15877.tif) (7224 x 4821) (104.5 MB)

Caption:

This photograph shows the Vehicle System Test Bed (VSTB) rover, a nearly identical copy to the Curiosity rover on Mars. The VSTB is used for Earth based testing and is shown during night time mobility testing in the Marsyard. Photo by Dan Goods and David Delgado.
