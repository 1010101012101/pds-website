# Wind Erosion * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15310_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15310.jpg) (1350 x 2771) (520.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15310.tif) (1350 x 2771) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The unusual surface texture seen in the VIS image reflects the resistance of the surface rocks to erosion by the wind. This image shows part of the northern end of Gordii Dorsum.

Orbit Number: 44508 Latitude: 12.3231 Longitude: 212.46 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-12-27 01:46
