# Dark Slope Streaks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15307_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15307.jpg) (1327 x 2764) (321.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15307.tif) (1327 x 2764) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Dark streaks mark the sides of this mesa where the movement of material has exposed the darker rock beneath. This mesa (or hill) is just one of hundreds in Tartarus Colles.

Orbit Number: 44484 Latitude: 22.9152 Longitude: 185.841 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-12-25 02:17
