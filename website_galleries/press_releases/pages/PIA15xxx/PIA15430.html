# Polar Clouds * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15430_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15430.jpg) (1165 x 2785) (234.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15430.tif) (1165 x 2785) (3.2 MB)

Caption:


Context image

With the changing of seasons comes changes in weather. This VIS image shows clouds in the north polar region. The surface is just barely visible in part of the image.

Orbit Number: 44858 Latitude: 74.3324 Longitude: 211.11 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2012-01-24 20:49
