# Boundaries * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15358_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15358.jpg) (1327 x 2782) (483.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15358.tif) (1327 x 2782) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This image shows a clear boundary between material being eroded by the wind (bottom of image) and a surface scoured clean (top of frame). The wind eroded material is part of a large deposit northwest of Apollinaris Mons.

Orbit Number: 44710 Latitude: -6.17171 Longitude: 174.354 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2012-01-12 14:55
