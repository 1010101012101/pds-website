# Arsia Mons 'Ripples' * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15364_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15364.jpg) (1324 x 2777) (477.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15364.tif) (1324 x 2777) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

A series of low, concentric ridges is located to the west of Arsia Mons. The origin of these features is unknown, and there are no similar features at the other Tharsis volcanoes.
