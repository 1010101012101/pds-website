# Layers in Burns Cliff Examined by Opportunity * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15091_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15091.jpg) (862 x 647) (112.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15091.tif) (862 x 647) (1.7 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity studied layers in the Burns Cliff slope of Endurance Crater in 2004. The layers show different types of deposition of sulfate-rich sediments. Opportunity's panoramic camera recorded this image.
