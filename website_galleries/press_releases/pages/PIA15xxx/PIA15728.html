# Tractus Catena * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15728_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15728.jpg) (1340 x 2916) (249.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15728.tif) (1340 x 2916) (3.9 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Tractus Catena is a series of collapse pits and fractures south of Alba Mons. The collapse pits, which run in two directions in this image, are typically indicative of volcanic lava tubes. The paired fractures are tectonic features.
