# Gale Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15911_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15911.jpg) (688 x 1538) (93.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15911.tif) (688 x 1538) (1.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows the northwestern floor and rim of Gale crater. A channel dissects the rim in this image, and the edge of the central mound is visible in the bottom right corner. Gale Crater is the targeted location for the MSL lander that will shortly arrive at Mars.
