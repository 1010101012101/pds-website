# Elysium Mons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA15xxx/PIA15216_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA15216.jpg) (1382 x 2763) (559.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA15216.tif) (1382 x 2763) (3.8 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of the eastern flank of Elysium Mons. The small channels were likely created due to the flow of lava, rather than water.

Orbit Number: 44248 Latitude: 24.2191 Longitude: 151.509 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-12-05 16:06
