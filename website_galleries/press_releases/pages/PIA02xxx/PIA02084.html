# Martian Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02084_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02084.jpg) (1024 x 1536) (261.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02084.tif) (1024 x 1536) (1.5 MB)

Caption:

This crater on northern Elysium Planitia is a little more than twice the diameter of the famous Meteor Crater in Arizona, U.S.A. It formed by the impact and subsequent explosion of a meteorite. Picture from MOC in July 1998.
