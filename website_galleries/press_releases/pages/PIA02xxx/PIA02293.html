# Saturn's F-Ring * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02293_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02293.jpg) (270 x 359) (7.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02293.tif) (270 x 359) (19.1 kB)

Caption:

This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's F Ring was taken through the Clear filter while at a distance of 6.9 million km from Saturn on 8 November 1980. The brightness variations of this tightly-constrained ring shown here indicate that the ring is less uniform in makeup than the larger rings.

Background Info:

JPL managed the Voyager Project for NASA's Office of Space Science
