# D and D (Dunes and Devils) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02161_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02161.jpg) (512 x 1660) (67.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02161.tif) (512 x 1660) (851.0 kB)

Caption:

3 February 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows streaks created by late spring and early summer dust devils on a field of dark sand dunes on the floor of Hooke Crater.

Location near : 45.0°S, 44.8°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
