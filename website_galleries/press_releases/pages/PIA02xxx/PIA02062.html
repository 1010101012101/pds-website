# Observed Spectral Classes (TES) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02062_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02062.jpg) (2100 x 3000) (355.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02062.tif) (2100 x 3000) (213.4 kB)

Caption:

Surface, atmospheric dust, and atmospheric water-ice clouds have been observed as approximate spectral end-members as well as in combinations. Note the deep 15-micron line for atmospheric CO 2 and the near 7 micron water vapor lines.
