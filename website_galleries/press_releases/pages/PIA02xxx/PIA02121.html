# Bull's Eye! * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02121_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02121.jpg) (484 x 444) (7.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02121.tif) (484 x 444) (215.4 kB)

Caption:

This image shows the initial ejecta that resulted when NASA's Deep Impact probe collided with comet Tempel 1 at 10:52 p.m. Pacific time, July 3 (1:52 a.m. Eastern time, July 4). It was taken by the spacecraft's medium-resolution camera 16 seconds after impact.
