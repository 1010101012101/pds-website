# Saturn's Red Spot * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02239_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02239.jpg) (600 x 600) (93.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02239.tif) (600 x 600) (677.6 kB)

Caption:

This false color image, taken on November 6, 1980 from a distance of about 8 million kilometers, shows somewhat similar, although much smaller, red spot on Saturn. False color was used to make the faint spot more visible.
