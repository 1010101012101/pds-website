# Slope Processes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02083_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02083.jpg) (1256 x 1857) (475.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02083.tif) (1256 x 1857) (2.4 MB)

Caption:

These two buttes in the Elysium Basin exhibit many small dark streaks on their slopes. Each streak is the result of mass-movement (landslides). Darker streaks appear to be younger than the brighter ones. Image from MOC taken in April 1998.
