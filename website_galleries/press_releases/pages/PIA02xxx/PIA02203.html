# Limb clouds over Triton * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02203_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02203.jpg) (450 x 900) (19.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02203.tif) (450 x 900) (92.5 kB)

Caption:

Limb clouds over Triton's south polar cap. Image is stretched to enhance the limb clouds and surface features. The image shows the cloud on the west limb that extends about 100 km along the limb and appears detached over much of its length.
