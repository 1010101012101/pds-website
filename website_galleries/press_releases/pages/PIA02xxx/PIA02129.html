# Untouched Tempel * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02129_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02129.jpg) (480 x 480) (18.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02129.tif) (480 x 480) (230.9 kB)

Caption:

This image shows the view from Deep Impact's probe 30 seconds before it was pummeled by comet Tempel 1. The image was taken by the probe's impactor targeting sensor.
