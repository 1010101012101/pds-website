# Fretted Terrain Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02074_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02074.jpg) (1024 x 1514) (324.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02074.tif) (1024 x 1514) (1.5 MB)

Caption:

Some craters in the middle latitudes of Mars exhibit strange, concentric- and radial-textured patters on their floors--as seen here. The origin is presently unknown. This crater is in northern Arabia Terra, and the picture was taken in April 1998.
