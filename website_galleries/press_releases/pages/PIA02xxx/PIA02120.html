# Face-to-Face With a Comet * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02120_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02120.jpg) (247 x 240) (5.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02120.tif) (247 x 240) (59.6 kB)

Caption:

This image shows comet Tempel 1 sixty seconds before it ran over NASA's Deep Impact probe at 10:52 p.m. Pacific time, July 3 (1:52 a.m. Eastern time, July 4). The picture was taken by the probe's impactor targeting sensor.
