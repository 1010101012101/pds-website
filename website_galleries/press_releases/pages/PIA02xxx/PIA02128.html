# So Close You Can Almost Touch It * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02128_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02128.jpg) (494 x 494) (21.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02128.tif) (494 x 494) (244.5 kB)

Caption:

This image shows the view from Deep Impact's probe 90 seconds before it was pummeled by comet Tempel 1. The image was taken by the probe's impactor targeting sensor.
