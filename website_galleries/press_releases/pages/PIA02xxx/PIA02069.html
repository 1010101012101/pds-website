# Frost-covered Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02069_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02069.jpg) (706 x 1544) (139.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02069.tif) (706 x 1544) (874.8 kB)

Caption:

MOC image of dunes in Chasma Boreale, a giant trough in the north polar cap. This September 1998 view shows dark sand emergent from beneath a veneer of bright frost left over from the northern winter that ended in July 1998.
