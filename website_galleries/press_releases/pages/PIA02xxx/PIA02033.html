# Regional Topographic Model of the Hellas Basin * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02033_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02033.jpg) (1909 x 2353) (901.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02033.tif) (1909 x 2353) (8.5 MB)

Caption:

Regional topographic model of the Hellas basin. (Top) Azimuthally-averaged radial topography used in the calculation of infilling the basin with surrounding material postulated to have been excavated from it. (Bottom) Color-coded topography plotted in an equal-area projection. The black lines correspond to zero-elevation contours.
