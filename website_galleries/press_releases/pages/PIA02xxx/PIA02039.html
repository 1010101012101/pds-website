# Elevations Within the Floor of the Valles Marineris * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02039_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02039.jpg) (2545 x 2023) (1.0 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02039.tif) (2545 x 2023) (7.4 MB)

Caption:

Elevations within the floor of the Valles Marineris canyon system and the adjacent Chryse outflow channels. All areas not color contoured have elevations above -1.9 km.
