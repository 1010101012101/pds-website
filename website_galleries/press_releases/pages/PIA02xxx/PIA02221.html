Caption:

This photo of Triton is one of a continuing series of "observatory phase" images obtained by the Voyager spacecraft. Lines inscribed on the image at right form a reference grid used by the Imaging Science Team.

Background Info:

The Voyager project is managed by the Jet Propulsion Laboratory for the NASA Office of Space Science and Applications.
