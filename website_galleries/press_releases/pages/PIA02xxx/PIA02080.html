# Olympus Lava Flows * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02080_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02080.jpg) (717 x 1059) (156.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02080.tif) (717 x 1059) (750.0 kB)

Caption:

This view of the lower south flank of the Olympus Mons volcano shows lava flows with leveed central channels and a variety of surface textures. The picture was taken in July 1998.
