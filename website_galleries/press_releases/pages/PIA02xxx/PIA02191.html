# Comet Ejecta in Aerogel * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02191_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02191.jpg) (1547 x 1136) (117.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02191.tif) (1547 x 1136) (5.3 MB)

Caption:


Click on image for annotated image

This image shows a particle impact on the aluminum frame that holds the aerogel tiles. The debris from the impact shot into the adjacent aerogel tile producing the explosion pattern of ejecta framents captured in the material. A nice cratering experiment.
