# North Polar Sand Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02068_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02068.jpg) (1024 x 1375) (284.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02068.tif) (1024 x 1375) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

Dunes composed of low albedo (dark) sand grains encircle the north polar cap of Mars. This view was taken during the northern summer in May 1999.
