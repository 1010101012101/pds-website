# Hrad Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02076_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02076.jpg) (717 x 1400) (169.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02076.tif) (717 x 1400) (970.4 kB)

Caption:

Image shows a fluid-scoured surface in the Hrad Vallis system, located northwest of Elysium Mons. The fluid is presumed to have been water. Image obtained by MOC on July 20, 1998.
