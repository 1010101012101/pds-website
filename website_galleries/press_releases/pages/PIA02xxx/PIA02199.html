# Kidney-Shaped Impact * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02199_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02199.jpg) (1024 x 2848) (530.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02199.tif) (1024 x 2848) (2.9 MB)

Caption:

23 February 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows an unusally-shaped (not circular) impact crater in the Elysium region of Mars. A dark-toned lava flow surface is seen in the southern (lower) portion of the image.

Location near : 5.9°N, 220.0°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Winter
