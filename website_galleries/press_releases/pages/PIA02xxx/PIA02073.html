# Northern Plains * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02073_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02073.jpg) (1024 x 1514) (411.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02073.tif) (1024 x 1514) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

The northern plains of Mars exhibit craters that often appear to be partly buried by or exhumed from beneath layers of pitted and eroded material. This example was taken by MOC in August 1998.
