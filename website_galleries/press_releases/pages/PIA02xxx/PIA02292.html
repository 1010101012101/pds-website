# Saturn's F-Ring * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02292_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02292.jpg) (800 x 600) (58.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02292.tif) (800 x 600) (115.0 kB)

Caption:

This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's F Ring was taken through the Clear filter while at a distance of 0.75 million km from Saturn on 12 November 1980. The kinks and braids of this tightly-constrained ring are visible along with the outer edge of the A Ring.

Background Info:

JPL managed the Voyager Project for NASA's Office of Space Science.
