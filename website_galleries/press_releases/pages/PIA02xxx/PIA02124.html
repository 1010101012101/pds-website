# Before the Crash * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02124_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02124.jpg) (256 x 256) (4.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02124.tif) (256 x 256) (65.8 kB)

Caption:

This image shows comet Tempel 1 six minutes before it ran over NASA's Deep Impact probe at 10:52 a.m. Pacific time, July 3 (1:52 a.m. Eastern time, July 4). The picture was taken by the probe's impactor targeting sensor.
