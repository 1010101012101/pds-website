# Lineated Valley Floor * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02075_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02075.jpg) (737 x 1510) (306.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02075.tif) (737 x 1510) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

Valley floors in the middle latitudes, particularly in the "fretted terrain" of northern Arabia Terra (shown here) have curious grooved and pitted surfaces. These features not glaciers and show very little evidence (if any) of flow. Their origin is unknown. MOC image from July 1998.
