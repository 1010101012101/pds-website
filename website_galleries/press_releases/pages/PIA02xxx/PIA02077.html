# Valley Networks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02077_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02077.jpg) (717 x 1399) (188.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02077.tif) (717 x 1399) (1.0 MB)

Caption:

Small valleys in the martian cratered highlands are often quite old and have been modified by erosion and wind action. The valleys shown here are located on a crater rim in Terra Tyrrhena. MOC took this picture in April 1999.
