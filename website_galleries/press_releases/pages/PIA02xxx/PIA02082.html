# Alba Patera Surface * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02082_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02082.jpg) (614 x 813) (127.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02082.tif) (614 x 813) (566.4 kB)

Caption:

The lava flows and channels on the flanks of the Alba Patera volcano are mostly obscured by a covering of eroded, lumpy-textured material of unknown origin. This picture was taken in July 1998.
