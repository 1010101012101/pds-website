# Martian Streaker * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA02xxx/PIA02920_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA02920.jpg) (1024 x 1440) (255.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA02920.tif) (1024 x 1440) (1.5 MB)

Caption:

16 March 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a dust devil producing a track among dozens of other, preexisting streaks on a dusty, south middle-latitude plain on Mars. The dust devil is located just above (north/northwest of) a small, dark-floored crater.

Location near : 58.7°S, 141.1°
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
