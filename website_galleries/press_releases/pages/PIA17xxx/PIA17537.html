# Channels * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA17xxx/PIA17537_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA17537.jpg) (1422 x 2927) (407.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA17537.tif) (1422 x 2927) (4.2 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This image shows several of the channels located in the Elysium Mons volcanic complex. It is likely that these channels were formed by lava flow rather than water.
