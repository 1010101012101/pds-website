# Resistent Ejecta * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA17xxx/PIA17856_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA17856.jpg) (1377 x 2926) (492.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA17856.tif) (1377 x 2926) (4.0 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image of southern Amazonis Planitia shows two craters with ejecta that stand out above the surrounding surface. The ejecta has remained in place while the surrounding material was removed. Extensive wind erosion is common in this region.
