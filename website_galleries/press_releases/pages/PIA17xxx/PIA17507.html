# Rabe Crater Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA17xxx/PIA17507_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA17507.jpg) (320 x 3600) (168.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA17507.tif) (320 x 3600) (1.2 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This IR image shows part of the dune field on the floor of Rabe Crater. The dunes are "brighter" than the surrounding material, indicating that they are warmer. In visible wavelength images the dunes are dark, because they are comprised of basaltic sand.
