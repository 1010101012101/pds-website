# Ravi Vallis Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA17xxx/PIA17546_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA17546.jpg) (1365 x 2928) (388.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA17546.tif) (1365 x 2928) (4.0 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows another portion of Ravi Vallis (see PIA17545 ). In this image a small crater and the resistant material formed during the impact form a "donut" on the floor of the valley.

Orbit Number: 52058 Latitude: -0.104303 Longitude: 319.153 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2013-09-08 10:35
