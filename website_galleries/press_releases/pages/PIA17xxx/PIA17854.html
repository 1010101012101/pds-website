# Patapsco Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA17xxx/PIA17854_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA17854.jpg) (1422 x 2923) (353.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA17854.tif) (1422 x 2923) (4.2 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows where Patapsco Vallis enters the largest depression of Elysium Fossae. Patapsco Vallis, as other valley systems on the Elysium Mons volcanic complex, was likely formed by the flow of lava rather than water.
