# Mars Interior (Artist Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16078_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16078.jpg) (5760 x 6220) (1.6 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16078.tif) (5760 x 6220) (107.5 MB)

Caption:

Artist rendition of the formation of rocky bodies in the solar system -- how they form and differentiate and evolve into terrestrial planets.

Background Info:

For more information, see http://insight.jpl.nasa.gov/ .

Photojournal Note : After thorough examination, NASA managers have decided to suspend the planned March 2016 launch of the Interior Exploration using Seismic Investigations Geodesy and Heat Transport (InSight) mission. The decision follows unsuccessful attempts to repair a leak in a section of the prime instrument in the science payload.
