# Landslides * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16265_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16265.jpg) (1305 x 2802) (379.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16265.tif) (1305 x 2802) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Several landslide deposits are visible in this VIS image of an unnamed crater in Terra Cimmeria. The bowl shaped depressions near the top of the crater rim are where the landslides started.

Orbit Number: 49355 Latitude: -36.2236 Longitude: 147.403 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2013-01-28 22:31
