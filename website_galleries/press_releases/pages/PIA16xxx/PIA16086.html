# Part of Curiosity's Outstretched Arm, Full-Resolution * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16086_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16086.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (103.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16086.tif) (1024 x 1024) (1.1 MB)

Caption:

This full-resolution image from NASA's Curiosity shows the elbow joint of the rover's extended robotic arm on Aug. 20, 2012. The Navigation Camera captured this view.

Background Info:

JPL manages the Mars Science Laboratory/Curiosity for NASA's Science Mission Directorate in Washington. The rover was designed, developed and assembled at JPL, a division of the California Institute of Technology in Pasadena.

For more about NASA's Curiosity mission, visit: http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/msl , http://www.nasa.gov/mars , and http://marsprogram.jpl.nasa.gov/msl .
