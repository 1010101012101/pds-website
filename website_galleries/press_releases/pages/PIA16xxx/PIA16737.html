# Asteroid 2012 DA14 as Seen from Siding Spring, Australia * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16737_med.gif) for a larger view * [Movie Download Options](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/animation/PIA16737) * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16737.jpg) (383 x 710) (26.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16737.tif) (383 x 710) (272.4 kB)

Caption:


Click on the image for the animation

This animated set of images, from the telescope known as the iTelescope.net Siding Spring Observatory , shows asteroid 2012 DA14 as the streak moving from top to bottom in the field of view. The images were taken around 9:40 a.m. PST (12:40 p.m. EST, or 17:40 UTC) on Feb. 15, 2013.

The exposure time was 5 seconds long.
