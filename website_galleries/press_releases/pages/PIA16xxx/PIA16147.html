# Flexing Curiosity's Arm * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16147_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16147.jpg) (1427 x 578) (116.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16147.tif) (1427 x 578) (2.5 MB)

Caption:

This engineering drawing shows the arm on NASA's Curiosity's rover in its "ready-for-action" position, or "ready out" as engineers say, in addition to the position it assumes to drop off samples.
