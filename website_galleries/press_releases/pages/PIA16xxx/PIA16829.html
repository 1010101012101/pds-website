# Fireball Flash over Russia * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16829_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16829.jpg) (1920 x 1280) (199.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16829.tif) (1920 x 1280) (7.4 MB)

Caption:

This image shows the flash above Chelyabinsk, Russia, from the fireball streaking through the sky on Feb. 15, 2013. The small asteroid was approximately 56 to 66 feet (17 to 20 meters) in diameter. The picture was taken by a local, M. Ahmetvaleev.
