# Gullies * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16266_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16266.jpg) (1324 x 2819) (292.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16266.tif) (1324 x 2819) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Several gullies of different sizes are located on the southern rim of this unnamed crater in Terra Cimmeria. The gullies form on the north-facing rim which gets more sun related warming than the south-facing rim.
