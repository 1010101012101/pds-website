# Fireball Streaking over Russia * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16828_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16828.jpg) (3029 x 1080) (382.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16828.tif) (3029 x 1080) (9.8 MB)

Caption:

This photograph of the meteor streaking through the sky above Chelyabinsk, Russia, on Feb. 15, 2013, was taken by a local, M. Ahmetvaleev. The small asteroid was about 56 to 66 feet (17 to 20 meters) wide.
