# Elysium Channels * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA16xxx/PIA16310_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA16310.jpg) (1369 x 2773) (313.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA16310.tif) (1369 x 2773) (3.8 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The channels in this VIS image are located on the northwestern margin of the Elysium Volcanic complex. Channels are very common in this region and were likely created by the flow of lava.

Orbit Number: 46895 Latitude: 30.9702 Longitude: 139.242 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2012-07-10 13:03
