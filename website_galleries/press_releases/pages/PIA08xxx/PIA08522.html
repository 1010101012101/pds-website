# Travelling Companions * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08522_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08522.jpg) (672 x 960) (157.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08522.tif) (672 x 960) (646.0 kB)

Caption:

8 June 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a large dust devil flanked by two relatively small dust devils, moving together across a lightly-dusted, south high-latitude plain.

Location near : 65.6°S, 20.6°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
