# Slam! * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08637_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08637.jpg) (601 x 1972) (256.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08637.tif) (601 x 1972) (1.2 MB)

Caption:

2 August 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows an impact crater on the martian northern plains. This crater is roughly the size of the famous Meteor Crater in Arizona on the North American continent.

Location near : 43.0°N, 231.7°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Spring
