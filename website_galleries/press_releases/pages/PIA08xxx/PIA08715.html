# Windy Work * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08715_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08715.jpg) (836 x 1597) (273.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08715.tif) (836 x 1597) (1.3 MB)

Caption:

30 August 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows crescent-shaped, scooped-out hollows where wind has eroded the local bedrock in the Apollinaris Sulci region.

Location near : 11.8°S, 179.5°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Southern Summer
