# Spallanzani Layers * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08027_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08027.jpg) (672 x 1108) (57.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08027.tif) (672 x 1108) (745.6 kB)

Caption:

31 March 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a layered, light-toned mesa among other layered materials exposed in a mound that covers much of the floor of Spallanzani Crater.

Location near : 58.3°S, 273.9°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
