# Ripple Belt * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08607_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08607.jpg) (1536 x 4352) (606.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08607.tif) (1536 x 4352) (6.7 MB)

Caption:

16 July 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows windblown materials that have collected and been shaped into large ripples in a valley in the Auqakuh Vallis system in northeastern Arabia Terra, Mars.

Location near : 29.1°N, 299.6°W
Image width : ~2 km (~1.2 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Winter
