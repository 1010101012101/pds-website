Caption:

5 August 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a small, dust-mantled volcano on the plains east of the giant martian volcano, Pavonis Mons.

Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
