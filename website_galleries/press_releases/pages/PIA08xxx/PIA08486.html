# Breaking Trail * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08486_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08486.jpg) (672 x 996) (76.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08486.tif) (672 x 996) (670.2 kB)

Caption:

25 May 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows gullies in the north wall of a crater south of Proctor Crater in Noachis Terra. To form, the gullies might have required liquid water. Dark streaks cutting across the scene were formed by passing dust devils.

Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
