# Dark Spots * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08658_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08658.jpg) (604 x 460) (51.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08658.tif) (604 x 460) (278.4 kB)

Caption:

Dark spots (left) and 'fans' appear to scribble dusty hieroglyphics on top of the Martian south polar cap in two high-resolution Mars Global Surveyor, Mars Orbiter Camera images taken in southern spring. Each image is about 3-kilometers wide (2-miles).
