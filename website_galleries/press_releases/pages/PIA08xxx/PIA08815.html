# Getting a Sense of Scale * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08815_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08815.jpg) (1800 x 1800) (218.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08815.tif) (1800 x 1800) (9.7 MB)

Caption:

This photo composite shows an aerial view of FedEx Field in Landover, Md., home of the Washington Redskins, superimposed on Mars' Victoria Crater to give a sense of the crater's scale.
