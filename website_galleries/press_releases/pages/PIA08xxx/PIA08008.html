# Noachis Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08008_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08008.jpg) (1024 x 1664) (163.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08008.tif) (1024 x 1664) (1.7 MB)

Caption:

22 March 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows dark sand dunes and relatively small, light-toned, windblown ripples on the floor of a crater in central Noachis Terra.

Location near : 50.0°S, 353.7°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Autumn
