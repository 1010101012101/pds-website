# Incomplete Puzzle * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA08xxx/PIA08076_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA08076.jpg) (1024 x 1926) (249.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA08076.tif) (1024 x 1926) (2.0 MB)

Caption:

15 April 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a mid-summer view of a portion of the south polar residual cap of Mars. The large, relatively flat-lying, puzzle-like pieces in this scene are mesas composed largely of solid carbon dioxide.

Location near : 85.5°S, 76.8°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
