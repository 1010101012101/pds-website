# Powered Landing of Phoenix (Artist Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA09xxx/PIA09943_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA09943.jpg) (5200 x 4800) (1.7 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA09943.tif) (5200 x 4800) (74.9 MB)

Caption:

This artist's concept depicts NASA's Phoenix Mars Lander a moment before its 2008 touchdown on the arctic plains of Mars. Pulsed rocket engines control the spacecraft's speed during the final seconds of descent.

Background Info:

Photojournal Note: As planned, the Phoenix lander, which landed May 25, 2008 23:53 UTC, ended communications in November 2008, about six months after landing, when its solar panels ceased operating in the dark Martian winter.
