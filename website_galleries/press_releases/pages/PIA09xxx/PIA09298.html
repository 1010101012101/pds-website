# Windstreak * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA09xxx/PIA09298_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA09298.jpg) (1361 x 2932) (506.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA09298.tif) (1361 x 2932) (4.0 MB)

Caption:


Context image for PIA09298
Windstreak

The windstreaks in this image are located of Arsia Mons lava flows in the Daedalia Planum region.

Image information: VIS instrument. Latitude -12.0N, Longitude 223.8°E. 18 meter/pixel resolution.

Note: this THEMIS visual image has not been radiometrically nor geometrically calibrated for this preliminary release. An empirical correction has been performed to remove instrumental effects. A linear shift has been applied in the cross-track and down-track direction to approximate spacecraft and planetary motion. Fully calibrated and geometrically projected images will be released through the Planetary Data System in accordance with Project policies at a later time.
