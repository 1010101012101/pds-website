# Bright and Dark * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA09xxx/PIA09139_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA09139.jpg) (1432 x 2925) (644.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA09139.tif) (1432 x 2925) (4.2 MB)

Caption:


Context image for PIA09139
Bright and Dark

East of Adamas Labyrinthus is this region with a stark contrast between bright and dark surfaces.

Image information: VIS instrument. Latitude 36.1°N, Longitude 109.2°E. 19 meter/pixel resolution.

Note: this THEMIS visual image has not been radiometrically nor geometrically calibrated for this preliminary release. An empirical correction has been performed to remove instrumental effects. A linear shift has been applied in the cross-track and down-track direction to approximate spacecraft and planetary motion. Fully calibrated and geometrically projected images will be released through the Planetary Data System in accordance with Project policies at a later time.
