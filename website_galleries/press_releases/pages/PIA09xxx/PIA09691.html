# Opportunity's Path, Sol 1,215 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA09xxx/PIA09691_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA09691.jpg) (3240 x 2380) (432.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA09691.tif) (3240 x 2380) (23.1 MB)

Caption:

The route followed by NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity from its landing site through the 1,215th Martian day (or sol) of its mission (June 24, 2007), is marked on this map. The underlying image is from the Mars Observer Camera on NASA's Mars Global Surveyor orbiter.
