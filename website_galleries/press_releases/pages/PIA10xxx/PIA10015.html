# Shutterbugs Shoot "Scarecrow" * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA10xxx/PIA10015_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA10015.jpg) (4288 x 2848) (1.5 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA10015.tif) (4288 x 2848) (36.6 MB)

Caption:

Onlookers watch as Scarecrow, a mobility-testing model for NASA's Mars Science Laboratory, easily conquers boulders in the Mars Yard testing area at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

Background Info:

The Mars Science Laboratory rover is in development for launch in 2009. JPL, a division of the California Institute of Technology, Pasadena, manages the mission for the NASA Science Mission Directorate, Washington.
