# Draining the Highland * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA10xxx/PIA10038_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA10038.jpg) (1182 x 2773) (606.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA10038.tif) (1182 x 2773) (3.3 MB)

Caption:


Context image for PIA10038
Draining the Highland

Drainage channels completely fill this image. The flow is toward the bottom of the frame, from the highland of Claritas Fossae toward the lowlands of Icaria Planum.

Image information: VIS instrument. Latitude 0.0°N, Longitude 0.0°E. 0 meter/pixel resolution.

Note: this THEMIS visual image has not been radiometrically nor geometrically calibrated for this preliminary release. An empirical correction has been performed to remove instrumental effects. A linear shift has been applied in the cross-track and down-track direction to approximate spacecraft and planetary motion. Fully calibrated and geometrically projected images will be released through the Planetary Data System in accordance with Project policies at a later time.
