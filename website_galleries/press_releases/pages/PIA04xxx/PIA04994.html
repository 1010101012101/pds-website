# Facing the Sun * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04994_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04994.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (13.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04994.tif) (1024 x 1024) (81.1 kB)

Caption:

Click for larger view

The bright white dot in this image taken by the panoramic camera onboard the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit is the Sun, used to help point the rover's high-gain antenna toward Earth. The inset shows the Sun magnified five times.
