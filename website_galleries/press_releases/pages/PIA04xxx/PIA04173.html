# Frosty Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04173_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04173.jpg) (512 x 1298) (171.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04173.tif) (512 x 1298) (665.5 kB)

Caption:

29 August 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows frost-covered sand dunes in the martian north polar region. The winds responsible for these dunes generally blew from the southwest (lower left).

Location near : 80.0°N, 114.6°W
Image width : width: ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Spring
