# First IMP Image * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04310_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04310.jpg) (256 x 248) (9.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04310.tif) (256 x 248) (22.1 kB)

Caption:

This is the first image from the Imager for Mars Pathfinder (IMP) that includes a glimpse of the plant surface. This image was taken at 8:42 MLSTsol 1.

Background Info:

Photojournal note: Sojourner spent 83 days of a planned seven-day mission exploring the Martian terrain, acquiring images, and taking chemical, atmospheric and other measurements. The final data transmission received from Pathfinder was at 10:23 UTC on September 27, 1997. Although mission managers tried to restore full communications during the following five months, the successful mission was terminated on March 10, 1998.
