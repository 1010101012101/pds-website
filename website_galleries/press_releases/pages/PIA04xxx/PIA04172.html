# Layered Slope * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04172_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04172.jpg) (836 x 1940) (252.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04172.tif) (836 x 1940) (1.6 MB)

Caption:

28 August 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a frost-covered slope in the south polar region of Mars. The layered nature of the terrain in the south polar region is evident in a series of irregular, somewhat stair-stepped bands that run across the image.
