# Utopia Cracks and Polygons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04476_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04476.jpg) (540 x 810) (99.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04476.tif) (540 x 810) (438.1 kB)

Caption:

MGS MOC Release No. MOC2-339, 23 April 2003

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a pattern of polygonal cracks and aligned, elliptical pits in western Utopia Planitia. The picture covers an area about 3 km (about 1.9 mi) wide near 44.9°N, 274.7°W. Sunlight illuminates the scene from the left.
