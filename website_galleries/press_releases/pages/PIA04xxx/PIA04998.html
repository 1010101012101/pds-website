# Spirit's Airbags Leave Trail * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04998_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04998.jpg) (640 x 480) (31.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04998.tif) (640 x 480) (835.9 kB)

Caption:

The smooth surfaces of angular and rounded rocks seen in this image of the martian terrain may be the result of wind-polishing debris. The picture was taken by the panoramic camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit.
