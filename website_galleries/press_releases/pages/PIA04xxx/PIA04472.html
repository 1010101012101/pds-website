# Grooved Herschel Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04472_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04472.jpg) (540 x 810) (80.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04472.tif) (540 x 810) (438.1 kB)

Caption:

MGS MOC Release No. MOC2-335, 19 April 2003

This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image portrays the dark, somewhat cemented, wind-scoured sand dunes of central Herschel Crater. The picture covers an area about 3 km (1.9 mi) wide near 15.7°S, 228.8°W. Sunlight illuminates the scene from the upper left.
