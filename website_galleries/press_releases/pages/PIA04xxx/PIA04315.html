# The Second Rover Image * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04315_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04315.jpg) (768 x 480) (53.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04315.tif) (768 x 480) (358.2 kB)

Caption:

This image of the Mars Pathfinder Lander on the surface of Mars was imaged by Sojourner as it looks back. Sojourners tracks are visible in the foreground.

Background Info:

Photojournal note: Sojourner spent 83 days of a planned seven-day mission exploring the Martian terrain, acquiring images, and taking chemical, atmospheric and other measurements. The final data transmission received from Pathfinder was at 10:23 UTC on September 27, 1997. Although mission managers tried to restore full communications during the following five months, the successful mission was terminated on March 10, 1998.
