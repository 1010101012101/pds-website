# Collapse Pits * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04122_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04122.jpg) (2048 x 4144) (816.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04122.tif) (2048 x 4144) (8.5 MB)

Caption:

27 July 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a 1.5 meters per pixel (~5 ft/pixel) view of aligned pits formed by collapse along a fault trend in the Tractus Catena region of Mars, in northern Tharsis.
