# Martian Gullies * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04146_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04146.jpg) (2048 x 2944) (761.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04146.tif) (2048 x 2944) (6.0 MB)

Caption:

13 August 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows gullies cut into layered rock and debris on the wall of a south middle-latitude crater. Gullies such as these are common at middle latitudes and may have required water to form.
