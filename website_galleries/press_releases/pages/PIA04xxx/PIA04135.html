# Windblown Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04135_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04135.jpg) (1024 x 1661) (103.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04135.tif) (1024 x 1661) (1.7 MB)

Caption:

6 August 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a suite of small, dark sand dunes on the floor of a crater in western Arabia Terra. The winds responsible for these dunes generally blow from the east/southeast (right/lower right).

Location near : 6.3°N, 349.6°W
