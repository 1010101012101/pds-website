# Mars Exploration Rover, Vertical (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04928_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04928.jpg) (2500 x 3400) (734.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04928.tif) (2500 x 3400) (15.7 MB)

Caption:

December 15, 2003

An artist's concept portrays a NASA Mars Exploration Rover on the surface of Mars. Two rovers, Spirit and Opportunity, will reach Mars in January 2004. Each has the mobility and toolkit to function as a robotic geologist.

NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, Calif., manages the Mars Exploration Rover Project for the NASA Office of Space Science, Washington, D.C.
