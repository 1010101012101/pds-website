# First Color Image from Spirit * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04995_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04995.jpg) (3498 x 3851) (1.1 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04995.tif) (3498 x 3851) (25.6 MB)

Caption:

This is the first color image of Mars taken by the panoramic camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit. It is the highest resolution image ever taken on the surface of another planet.
