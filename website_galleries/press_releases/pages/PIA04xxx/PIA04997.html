# Wind-polished rocks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04997_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04997.jpg) (640 x 480) (36.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04997.tif) (640 x 480) (863.8 kB)

Caption:

The smooth surfaces of angular and rounded rocks seen in this image of the martian terrain may have been polished by wind-blown debris. The picture was taken by the panoramic camera on the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit.
