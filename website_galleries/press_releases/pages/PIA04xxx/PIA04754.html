# Delta Rocket Launch Carrying Mars Pathfinder * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04754_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04754.jpg) (1171 x 1500) (139.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04754.tif) (1171 x 1500) (3.9 MB)

Caption:

Mars Pathfinder was launched on a Delta Launch Vehicle at 1:56 am on 4 December 1996 from Cape Canaveral Spaceflight Center.

Background Info:

Photojournal note: Sojourner spent 83 days of a planned seven-day mission exploring the Martian terrain, acquiring images, and taking chemical, atmospheric and other measurements. The final data transmission received from Pathfinder was at 10:23 UTC on September 27, 1997. Although mission managers tried to restore full communications during the following five months, the successful mission was terminated on March 10, 1998.
