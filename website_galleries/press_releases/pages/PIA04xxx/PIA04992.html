# Right Panorama of Spirit's Landing Site * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA04xxx/PIA04992_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA04992.jpg) (1892 x 384) (65.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA04992.tif) (1892 x 384) (416.0 kB)

Caption:

This is a version of the first 3-D stereo image from the rover's navigation camera, showing only the view from the right stereo camera onboard the Mars Exploration Rover Spirit. The left and right camera images are combined to produce a 3-D image.
