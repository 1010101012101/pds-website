Caption:

This is a view of Jupiter taken by Voyager 1. This image was taken through three color filters and recombined to produce the color image. This photo was assembled from three black and white negatives by the Image Processing Lab at Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

Background Info:

JPL manages and controls the Voyager project for NASA's Office of Space Science.
