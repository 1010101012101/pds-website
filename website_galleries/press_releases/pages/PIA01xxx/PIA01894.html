# Opportunity's View, Sol 959 (Cylindrical) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA01xxx/PIA01894_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA01894.jpg) (7688 x 1045) (961.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA01894.tif) (7688 x 1045) (8.0 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity used its navigation camera to take the images combined into this stereo view of the rover's surroundings on sol (or Martian day) 959 of its surface mission.

This view is presented as a cylindrical projection with geometric seam correction.
