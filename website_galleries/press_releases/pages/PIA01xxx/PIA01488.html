# Uranus' Upper Atmosphere * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA01xxx/PIA01488_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA01488.jpg) (2118 x 2618) (396.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA01488.tif) (2118 x 2618) (8.5 MB)

Caption:

This computer enhancement of a Voyager 2 image, emphasizes the high-level haze in Uranus' upper atmosphere. Clouds are obscured by the overlying atmosphere.

Background Info:

JPL manages and controls the Voyager project for NASA's Office of Space Science.
