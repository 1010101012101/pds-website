# Voyager Testing: 1976 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21732_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21732.jpg) (2156 x 2192) (672.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21732.tif) (2156 x 2192) (14.2 MB)

Caption:

This archival photo shows engineers working on vibration acoustics and pyro shock testing of NASA's Voyager on November 18, 1976.
