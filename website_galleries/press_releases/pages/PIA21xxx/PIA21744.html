# Voyager 2 Launch * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21744_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21744.jpg) (1821 x 2316) (321.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21744.tif) (1821 x 2316) (12.1 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Voyager 2 spacecraft launched atop its Titan/Centaur-7 launch vehicle from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida on August 20, 1977, at 10:29 a.m. local time.
