Caption:


Context image

Not all Martian sand dunes are located in craters. This VIS image shows dunes located on the plains of Terra Sirenum.

Orbit Number: 67736 Latitude: -71.7973 Longitude: 216.088 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2017-03-22 07:46
