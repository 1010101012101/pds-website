# Claritas Fossae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21004_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21004.jpg) (666 x 1426) (122.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21004.tif) (666 x 1426) (736.0 kB)

Caption:


Context image

The linear depressions in this VIS image are some of the numerous graben that make up Claritas Fossae. Graben form when two faults cause a block of material to be dropped to a lower elevation.
