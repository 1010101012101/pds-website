# Dust Devil Tracks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21501_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21501.jpg) (1054 x 2727) (365.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21501.tif) (1054 x 2727) (2.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image show dust devil tracks in Aonia Terra. As the dust devil moves along the surface it scours the dust and fine materials away, revealing the darker rocky surface below the dust.

Orbit Number: 66962 Latitude: -68.8221 Longitude: 241.346 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2017-01-17 13:13
