# Voyager Development Test Model * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21731_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21731.jpg) (1000 x 978) (130.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21731.tif) (1000 x 978) (2.9 MB)

Caption:

This archival photo shows the encapsulation of the Voyager Development Test Model at NASA's Kennedy Space Center's Eastern Test Range. The picture was taken on October 8, 1976.
