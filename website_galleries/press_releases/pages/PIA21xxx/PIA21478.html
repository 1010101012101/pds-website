# Voyager 2 Flight Hardware * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21478_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21478.jpg) (3053 x 2419) (574.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21478.tif) (3053 x 2419) (20.7 MB)

Caption:

This archival photo shows engineers at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory working on the 10-sided central structure, or "bus," of the Voyager 2 spacecraft on February 24,1977.
