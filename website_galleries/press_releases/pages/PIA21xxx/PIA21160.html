# Pavonis Mons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21160_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21160.jpg) (1344 x 2640) (308.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21160.tif) (1344 x 2640) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of the area where Pavonis Chasma intersects with Pavonis Fossae. It is a region of lava flows from Pavonis Mons and depressions likely formed by collapse into lava tubes and tectonic graben.
