# Dust Devil Tracks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21316_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21316.jpg) (544 x 1469) (122.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21316.tif) (544 x 1469) (778.6 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows dust devil tracks on the plains of Aonia Terra. As the dust devil moves across the surface it scours the fine dust particles, revealing the darker rock surface below.

Orbit Number: 66800 Latitude: -65.2605 Longitude: 239.338 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2017-01-04 04:52
