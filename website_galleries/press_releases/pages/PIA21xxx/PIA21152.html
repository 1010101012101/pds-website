# Palikir Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21152_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21152.jpg) (1330 x 2677) (309.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21152.tif) (1330 x 2677) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image is of Palikir Crater in Terra Sirenum. The inner rim of the crater is dissected with numerous gullies. In higher resolution images from other imagers these gullies are the location of changing linea, which appear to grow and retreat as seasons change.
