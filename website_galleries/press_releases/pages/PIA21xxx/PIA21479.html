# Voyager: Antenna Dish Construction * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21479_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21479.jpg) (2543 x 2022) (658.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21479.tif) (2543 x 2022) (15.4 MB)

Caption:

Engineers are engaged in the construction of a high gain antenna for one of the Voyager spacecraft in This archival photo taken on October 29, 1975.
