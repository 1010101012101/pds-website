# Voyager: Antenna Dish Construction * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21480_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21480.jpg) (2529 x 2029) (525.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21480.tif) (2529 x 2029) (14.7 MB)

Caption:

This archival photo shows an engineer working on the construction of a large, dish-shaped Voyager high-gain antenna. The picture was taken on July 9, 1976.
