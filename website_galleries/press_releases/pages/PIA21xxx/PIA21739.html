# Voyager 1's Launch Vehicle * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21739_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21739.jpg) (1760 x 2227) (319.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21739.tif) (1760 x 2227) (2.6 MB)

Caption:

The Titan/Centaur-6 launch vehicle was moved to Launch Complex 41 at Kennedy Space Center in Florida to complete checkout procedures in preparation for launch. The photo is dated January 1977. This launch vehicle carried Voyager 1 into space on September 5, 1977.
