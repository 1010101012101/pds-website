# South Polar Layers * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21325_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21325.jpg) (1005 x 2641) (315.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21325.tif) (1005 x 2641) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows the layering of the South Polar cap of Mars. The image is located at the margin between the cap ice and the surrounding plains (bottom of image).

Orbit Number: 66924 Latitude: -79.7235 Longitude: 100.914 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2017-01-14 10:14
