# Nirgal Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21324_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21324.jpg) (675 x 1420) (107.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21324.tif) (675 x 1420) (719.9 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a portion of Nirgal Vallis. A tributary of the channel is seen on the left side of the image, with the main channel crossing the image from left to right.

Orbit Number: 66860 Latitude: -27.2359 Longitude: 315.463 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2017-01-09 03:18
