# Sirenum Fossae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA21xxx/PIA21177_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA21177.jpg) (1321 x 2660) (250.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA21177.tif) (1321 x 2660) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows part of Sirenum Fossae, a large graben on the margin between Terra Sirenum and Daedalis Planum. Grabens are formed by the downward movement of material between parallel tectonic faults.

Orbit Number: 65672 Latitude: -25.6345 Longitude: 220.325 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-10-03 07:01
