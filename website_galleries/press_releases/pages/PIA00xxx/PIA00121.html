# Earth - Eastern Australia Coast * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA00xxx/PIA00121_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA00121.jpg) (452 x 746) (57.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA00121.tif) (452 x 746) (693.7 kB)

Caption:

This false color image of the Eastern Coast of Australia was obtained by the Galileo spacecraft at about 3:00 p.m. PST, Dec. 8, 1990, at a range of more than 35,000 miles. The color composite was made from images taken through the methane, red and green filters.
