# Transmission Spectrum of HAT-P-11b * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18839_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18839.jpg) (1893 x 1125) (139.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18839.tif) (1893 x 1125) (6.4 MB)

Caption:

A plot of the transmission spectrum for exoplanet HAT-P-11b, with data from NASA's Kepler, Hubble and Spitzer observatories combined. The results show a robust detection of water absorption in the Hubble data. Transmission spectra of selected atmospheric models are plotted for comparison.

Background Info:

More information about exoplanets and NASA's planet-finding program is at http://planetquest.jpl.nasa.gov .
