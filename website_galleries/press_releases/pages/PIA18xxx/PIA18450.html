# Launch Tower for LDSD * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18450_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18450.jpg) (3264 x 2448) (658.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18450.tif) (3264 x 2448) (24.0 MB)

Caption:

This image shows the tower from which the test vehicle for NASA's Low-Density Supersonic Decelerator (LDSD) will hang before a balloon lifts it to high altitudes. The rocket-powered test vehicle will reach near-space heights before testing landing technologies for future Mars missions. The test will occur at the U.S. Navy's Pacific Missile Range Facility in Kauai, Hawaii.

Background Info:

The Low-Density Supersonic Decelerator Project is managed by JPL for NASA's Space Technology Mission Directorate in Washington.
