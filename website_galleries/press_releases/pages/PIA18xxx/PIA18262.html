Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a portion of Olympus Rupes, part of the steep margin surrounding Olympus Mons. The term rupes means scarp.

Orbit Number: 54309 Latitude: 22.1556 Longitude: 228.416 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2014-03-12 16:04
