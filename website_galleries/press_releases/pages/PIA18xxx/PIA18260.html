Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows some of the dunes that are located near the north polar cap. This region of dunes is part of Siton Undae.

Orbit Number: 54300 Latitude: 75.5168 Longitude: 300.696 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2014-03-11 21:50
