# Tartarus Colles * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18127_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18127.jpg) (1416 x 2924) (248.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18127.tif) (1416 x 2924) (4.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The hills in this VIS image are part of Tartarus Colles. Several of the hills have dark slope streaks, believed to be formed by downslope removal of dust revealing the darker rock beneath.
