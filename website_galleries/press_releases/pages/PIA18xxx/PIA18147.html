# MESSENGER Near Hokusai Crater (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18147_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18147.jpg) (3000 x 2009) (641.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18147.tif) (3000 x 2009) (18.1 MB)

Caption:

A depiction of the MESSENGER spacecraft is shown passing near the crater Hokusai and its extensive system of rays. Both the monochrome and enhanced color views of Mercury were obtained during MESSENGER's second Mercury flyby.
