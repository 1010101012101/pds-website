# Galileo Over Io (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18176_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18176.jpg) (3014 x 2422) (597.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18176.tif) (3014 x 2422) (21.9 MB)

Caption:

Artist's rendering of NASA's Galileo spacecraft flying past Jupiter's moon Io. Galileo made multiple close approaches to the volcanically active moon during its time at Jupiter, including a first pass in Dec. 1995, during its arrival in the Jupiter system.
