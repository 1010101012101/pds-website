# MESSENGER Views Rachmaninoff Basin (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18149_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18149.jpg) (2400 x 1400) (280.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18149.tif) (2400 x 1400) (10.1 MB)

Caption:

A depiction of the MESSENGER spacecraft is shown viewing the Rachmaninoff basin. Both the monochrome and enhanced color views of Mercury were obtained during MESSENGER's third Mercury flyby.
