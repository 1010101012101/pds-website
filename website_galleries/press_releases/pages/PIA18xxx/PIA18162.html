# Lunar Prospector (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA18xxx/PIA18162_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA18162.jpg) (2000 x 1594) (373.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA18162.tif) (2000 x 1594) (9.6 MB)

Caption:

This artist's concept of the Lunar Prospector shows the spacecraft in lunar orbit. Instrument masts are fully deployed.

Lunar Prospector spent about a year and a half studying and mapping the Moon. After its mission was complete, was deliberately crashed into the Moon's south pole to look for evidence of water ice. None was observed.
