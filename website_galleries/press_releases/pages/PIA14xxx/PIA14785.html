# Juventae Chasma * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA14xxx/PIA14785_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA14785.jpg) (672 x 1464) (66.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA14785.tif) (672 x 1464) (985.0 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image is on the southwestern margin of Juventae Chasma. Located to the north of Marineris Vallis, this chasma is elongate in the north/south direction, rather than east/west.

Orbit Number: 43008 Latitude: -4.84971 Longitude: 296.607 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-08-25 12:46
