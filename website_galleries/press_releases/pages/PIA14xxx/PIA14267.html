# Special Delivery from Mars to Earth (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA14xxx/PIA14267_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA14267.jpg) (4976 x 4104) (1.3 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA14267.tif) (4976 x 4104) (61.3 MB)

Caption:

This artist's concept of a proposed Mars sample return mission portrays the separation of an Earth entry vehicle, bearing a container of Martian rock samples, from the main spacecraft that would have carried it from Martian orbit nearly to Earth.

Background Info:

NASA and the European Space Agency are collaborating on proposals for a mission to gather samples of Martian rocks and bring them to Earth after 2020. This illustration depicts preliminary concepts, not finished design.
