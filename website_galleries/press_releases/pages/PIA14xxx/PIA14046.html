# Eos Chaos * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA14xxx/PIA14046_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA14046.jpg) (1335 x 2933) (302.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA14046.tif) (1335 x 2933) (3.9 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The floor of Eos Chasma is composed of thousands of small hills. This type of terrain is called "chaos." Today's VIS image shows a small portion of Eos Chaos.

Orbit Number: 41098 Latitude: -43.6804 Longitude: 312.588 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-03-21 06:44
