# Tectonism * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA14xxx/PIA14970_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA14970.jpg) (1384 x 2801) (390.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA14970.tif) (1384 x 2801) (3.9 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Tectonic stresses deform and fracture rocks and planetary surfaces. Right angles are a good indication that the feature was formed by tectonic stresses. We see evidence of these stresses in today's VIS image of Margaritifer Terra.
