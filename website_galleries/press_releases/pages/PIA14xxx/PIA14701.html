Caption:

NASA's Dawn spacecraft obtained this image with its framing camera on Sept. 3, 2011. This image was taken through the camera's clear filter. The image has a resolution of about 220 meters per pixel.

Background Info:

More information about Dawn is online at http://www.nasa.gov/dawn and http://dawn.jpl.nasa.gov .
