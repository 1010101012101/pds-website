# Out of Round * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA14xxx/PIA14980_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA14980.jpg) (1372 x 2796) (311.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA14980.tif) (1372 x 2796) (3.8 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This unnamed crater in Terra Cimmeria is not as round in shape as other craters. Major landslides have occurred on the crater rim, resulting in the final shape we see today.
