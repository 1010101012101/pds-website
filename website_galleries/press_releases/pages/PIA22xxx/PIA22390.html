# Olympia Undae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22390_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22390.jpg) (1177 x 2604) (522.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22390.tif) (1177 x 2604) (3.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Sand dunes cover this entire VIS image. The dunes are part of Olympia Undae, a huge dune field surrounding 1/4 of the northern polar cap. This image was collected during northern summer, and the dunes are frost free.

Orbit Number: 71391 Latitude: 80.8754 Longitude: 198.41 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2018-01-17 12:12
