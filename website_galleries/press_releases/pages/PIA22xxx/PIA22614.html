# Daedalia Planum Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22614_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22614.jpg) (1353 x 2645) (284.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22614.tif) (1353 x 2645) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of an unnamed crater on the northwest margin of Daedaia Planum. Lava flows are visible around the crater rim. The interior of the crater has been filled by a material that is now being eroded to create the ridge and valley formation at the upper left part of the image.
