# Yuty Crater Ejecta * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22171_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22171.jpg) (711 x 1444) (132.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22171.tif) (711 x 1444) (799.1 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a portion of the ejecta deposit of Yuty Crater. The raised edge of the ejecta is termed a rampart, and indicates that a volatile like water may have been present. The ejecta edge looks like a flow front and much different from air fall ejecta emplacement.
