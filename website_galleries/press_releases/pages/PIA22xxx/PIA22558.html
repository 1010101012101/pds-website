# Optical Composite Image of Asteroid 2017 YE5 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22558_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22558.jpg) (1076 x 960) (115.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22558.tif) (1076 x 960) (967.3 kB)

Caption:

This optical composite image shows asteroid 2017 YE5, taken on June 30, 2018, by the Cadi Ayyad University Morocco Oukaimeden Sky Survey, one of the first surveys to identify 2017 YE5 in December 2017.

More information about asteroids and near-Earth objects is available at http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/asteroidwatch .
