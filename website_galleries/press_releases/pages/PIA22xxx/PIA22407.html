# Coprates Catena Landslide * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22407_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22407.jpg) (672 x 1419) (51.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22407.tif) (672 x 1419) (548.6 kB)

Caption:


Context image

A landslide deposit is visible in this VIS image of Coprates Catena. Coprates Catena parallels the much larger Coprates Chasma; both are part of Valles Marineris.

Orbit Number: 71880 Latitude: -14.7075 Longitude: 295.237 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2018-02-26 18:24
