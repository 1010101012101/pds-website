# Olympia Undae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22391_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22391.jpg) (1179 x 2590) (414.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22391.tif) (1179 x 2590) (3.0 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a small portion of Olympia Undae, a huge dune field that surrounds part of the north polar cap. This image was taken during summer and there is no frost on the dune forms.

Orbit Number: 71398 Latitude: 80.9837 Longitude: 139.963 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2018-01-18 01:56
