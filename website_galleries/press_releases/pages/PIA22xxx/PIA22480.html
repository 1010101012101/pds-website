# Mosaic of Cerealia Facula * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22480_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22480.jpg) (2461 x 1432) (247.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22480.tif) (2461 x 1432) (2.4 MB)

Caption:

This mosaic of Cerealia Facula combines images obtained from altitudes as low as 22 miles (35 km) above Ceres' surface. The mosaic is overlain on a topography model based on images obtained during Dawn's low altitude mapping orbit (240 miles or 385 km altitude). No vertical exaggeration was applied.

The center of Cerealia Facula is located at 19.7 degrees north latitude and 239.6 degrees south longitude.
