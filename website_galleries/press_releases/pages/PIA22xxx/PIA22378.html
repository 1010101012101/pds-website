# Bamberg Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22378_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22378.jpg) (721 x 1458) (86.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22378.tif) (721 x 1458) (757.3 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows the western rim of Bamberg Crater. The complex nature of the rim is one indication of the relative youth of this crater in relation to it's surrounding. Many gullies dissect this rim.

Orbit Number: 71254 Latitude: 39.6224 Longitude: 356.451 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2018-01-06 05:00
