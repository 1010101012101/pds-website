# Arabia Terra Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22370_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22370.jpg) (715 x 1449) (80.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22370.tif) (715 x 1449) (720.4 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows several craters. The interior of the central one has retained much of the original topography, including the central peak. The floor has not been smoothed by deposits of dust or other layering materials. These craters are located on the northern margin of Arabia Terra.
