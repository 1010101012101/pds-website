# Crater Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22618_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22618.jpg) (718 x 1458) (80.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22618.tif) (718 x 1458) (735.7 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image is located in the region between northern Terra Sabaea and Utopia Planitia. This unnamed crater contains a small dune field. The crater rim is heavily eroded and it appears that some downslope movement on the interior of the rim may have included a volatile component such as ice.
