# Pastuer Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA12xxx/PIA12851_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA12851.jpg) (1347 x 2769) (287.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA12851.tif) (1347 x 2769) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The small, dark features in this VIS image are sand dunes. The majority of the dunes appear to be located within eroded deposits on the floor of Pastuer Crater.
