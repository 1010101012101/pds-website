# Ceranus Tholus * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA12xxx/PIA12986_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA12986.jpg) (1326 x 2767) (293.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA12986.tif) (1326 x 2767) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows the western edge of the summit caldera of Ceranus Tholus, one of the smaller volcanic constructs of the Tharsis region. Several channels dissect the western flank of the volcano.

Orbit Number: 36045 Latitude: 24.1667 Longitude: 262.643 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-01-29 07:00
