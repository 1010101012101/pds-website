# Sandbox Testing to Prepare for Driving Spirit * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA12xxx/PIA12352_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA12352.jpg) (3456 x 2592) (1.2 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA12352.tif) (3456 x 2592) (26.9 MB)

Caption:

Rover-team members at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, Calif., check slight movements by a test rover during tests simulating the challenge of getting NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit out of a sand trap on Mars. From left: Alfonso Herrera, Matt Van Kirk, Mike Seibert, Brenda Franklin.

Background Info:

To see updates on the efforts to free the Spirit rover, visit the JPL Free Spirit website.
