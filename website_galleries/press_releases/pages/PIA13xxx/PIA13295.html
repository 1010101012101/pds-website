# Connecting Curiosity's "Neck" * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13295_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13295.jpg) (1917 x 1079) (231.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13295.tif) (1917 x 1079) (6.2 MB)

Caption:

In the clean room at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, engineers gather around the base of Curiosity's "neck" (the Mast) as they slowly lower it into place for attachment to the rover's "body" (the Wet Electronics Box, or "WEB").
