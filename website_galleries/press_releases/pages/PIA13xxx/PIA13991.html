# South Polar Clouds * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13991_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13991.jpg) (938 x 2771) (345.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13991.tif) (938 x 2771) (2.6 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Polar surface winds can reach high velocities. These winds can cause clouds to form when the winds flow into troughs and become chaotic. This VIS image shows trough clouds as linear bands.

Orbit Number: 40916 Latitude: -86.4592 Longitude: 100.949 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2011-03-06 07:28
