# North Polar Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13377_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13377.jpg) (1133 x 2752) (321.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13377.tif) (1133 x 2752) (3.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

As the sun warms the surface and more frost is removed, the dunes and other features near the north pole of Mars are revealed. This VIS image shows that the inter-dune areas are complex.

Orbit Number: 38296 Latitude: 79.184 Longitude: 143.729 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-08-02 14:58
