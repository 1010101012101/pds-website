# Eos Chasma * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13183_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13183.jpg) (1322 x 2784) (229.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13183.tif) (1322 x 2784) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

At eastern end of Valles Marineris is Eos Chasma. In Eos, the canyon system transitions into a region of chaos and then into major outflow channels. This image shows the transition into chaos.

Orbit Number: 36901 Latitude: 1.39403 Longitude: 45.6855 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-04-09 18:41
