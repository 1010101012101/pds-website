# Echus Chasma * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13251_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13251.jpg) (1335 x 2770) (507.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13251.tif) (1335 x 2770) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Echus Chasma separates the Tharsis region from Lunae Planum. This VIS image shows volcanic flow materials from Tharsis within the chasma.

Orbit Number: 37105 Latitude: 7.6562 Longitude: 281.476 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-04-26 13:04
