# Flying Under Comet Hartley 2 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13577_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13577.jpg) (2999 x 2249) (172.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13577.tif) (2999 x 2249) (6.8 MB)

Caption:

This image montage shows comet Hartley 2 as NASA's EPOXI mission approached and flew under the comet. The images progress in time clockwise, starting at the top left.

The image was taken by EPOXI's Medium-Resolution Instrument on Nov. 4, 2010. The sun is to the right.
