# Aurorae Chaos * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13407_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13407.jpg) (669 x 1461) (77.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13407.tif) (669 x 1461) (978.6 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Located at the eastern end of Vallis Marineris is the region of chaos called Aurorae. Today's VIS image is from the northern part of Aurorae Chaos and contains mesas separated by complex low lying regions.

Orbit Number: 38527 Latitude: -5.04156 Longitude: 325.171 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-08-21 13:58
