# Wind and Rock * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13633_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13633.jpg) (1356 x 2769) (386.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13633.tif) (1356 x 2769) (3.8 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows lava flows of Alba Mons and windstreaks behind craters in the area. Windstreak tail directions indicate winds from the East and East-Northeast.

Orbit Number: 39053 Latitude: 32.2875 Longitude: 246.438 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-10-03 23:14
