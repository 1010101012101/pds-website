# Floor of Tsiolkovskiy -- Constellation Region of Interest * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13089_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13089.jpg) (1042 x 969) (162.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13089.tif) (1042 x 969) (1.0 MB)

Caption:

LROC NAC view of boulders on an outlying rampart of the complex central peak of Tsiolkovskiy crater within the Constellation Region of Interest . The image is roughly 700 m wide (LROC NAC frame M113107391L)

Background Info:

NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center built and manages the mission for the Exploration Systems Mission Directorate at NASA Headquarters in Washington. The Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera was designed to acquire data for landing site certification and to conduct polar illumination studies and global mapping. Operated by Arizona State University, LROC consists of a pair of narrow-angle cameras (NAC) and a single wide-angle camera (WAC). The mission is expected to return over 70 terabytes of image data.
