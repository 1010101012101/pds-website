# Wind Effects * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13171_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13171.jpg) (1333 x 2772) (615.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13171.tif) (1333 x 2772) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Located on the margin of Zephyria Planum, today's image shows the effect the wind has had on the surface of Mars. Loose materials have been removed and semi-consolidated materials have been eroded into narrow hill forms.

Orbit Number: 36810 Latitude: 3.14977 Longitude: 151.047 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-04-02 06:52
