# Terra Sabaea * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13036_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13036.jpg) (672 x 1385) (95.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13036.tif) (672 x 1385) (931.9 kB)

Caption:


Context image

The northern part of Terra Sabaea is heavily fractured and channeled, breaking up into a chaotic terrain as the elevation drops down to the northern plains. This VIS image shows just one of the many channels located in the region.

Orbit Number: 36289 Latitude: 31.6379 Longitude: 64.8754 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-02-18 09:10
