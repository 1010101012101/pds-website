# Doublet Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13738_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13738.jpg) (1368 x 2920) (390.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13738.tif) (1368 x 2920) (4.0 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This doublet crater is located in Utopia Planitia, near the Elysium Volcanic region. Doublet craters are formed by simultaneous impact of a meteor that broke into two pieces prior to hitting the surface. The channels in the image are part of Granicus Valles.
