# Olympus Mons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA13xxx/PIA13713_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA13713.jpg) (1336 x 2770) (259.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA13713.tif) (1336 x 2770) (3.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of the northern escarpment of Olympus Mons. The semi-circular deposit on the left side of the frame is a large landslide deposit.

Orbit Number: 39478 Latitude: 23.5764 Longitude: 224.064 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2010-11-07 23:10
