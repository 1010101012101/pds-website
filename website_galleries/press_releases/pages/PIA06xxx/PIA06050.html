# Spirit's Shadow, Sol 153 (Left Eye) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06050_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06050.jpg) (2716 x 1338) (397.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06050.tif) (2716 x 1338) (2.6 MB)

Caption:

This is the left-eye half of a stereo pair of mosaics created from navigation camera images acquired by NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Spirit during Spirit's sol 153, on June 8, 2004. It is presented in a cylindrical-perspective projection. Spirit is pointing toward the base of the "Columbia Hills."

See PIA06049 for 3-D view and PIA06051 for right eye view of this left eye cylindrical-perspective projection.
