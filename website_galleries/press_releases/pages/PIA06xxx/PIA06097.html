# Cassini's First Picture of F Ring * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06097_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06097.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (30.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06097.tif) (1024 x 1024) (708.5 kB)

Caption:

This is one of the first images taken of Saturn's F ring by the Cassini spacecraft after it successfully entered Saturn's orbit. It was taken by the spacecraft's narrow angle camera and shows the sunlit side of the rings.
