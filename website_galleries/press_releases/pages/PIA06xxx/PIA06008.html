# Rippled Valley Floor * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06008_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06008.jpg) (1024 x 1854) (341.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06008.tif) (1024 x 1854) (1.9 MB)

Caption:

15 September 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a ripple-covered valley floor in the Hyblaeus Fossae region. Winds blowing up and down the length of the valley have helped to concentrate windblown grains to form these large, megaripples.
