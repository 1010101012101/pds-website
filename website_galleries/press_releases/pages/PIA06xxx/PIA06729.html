# Outcrop in Iani * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06729_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06729.jpg) (836 x 1254) (193.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06729.tif) (836 x 1254) (1.1 MB)

Caption:

28 July 2004
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a light-toned butte composed of layered, sedimentary rock in the Iani Chaos region of Mars. It is located near 1.6°S, 18.2°W. The picture covers an area about 3 km (1.9 mi) wide, and is illuminated by sunlight from the left.
