# Dunes in Noachis Terra * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06856_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06856.jpg) (672 x 3645) (370.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06856.tif) (672 x 3645) (2.5 MB)

Caption:

5 September 2004
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a field of dark, windblown sand dunes in the Noachis Terra region near 45.2°S, 321.4°W. The image covers an area approximately 3 km (1.9 mi) across. Sunlight illuminates this scene from the upper left.
