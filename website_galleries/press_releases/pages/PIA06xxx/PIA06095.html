# Rings Full of Waves * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06095_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06095.jpg) (768 x 768) (178.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06095.tif) (768 x 768) (410.9 kB)

Caption:

This image shows three density waves in Saturn's A ring. It was taken by the narrow angle camera on the Cassini spacecraft after successful entry into Saturn's orbit. The view shows the dark, or unlit, side of the rings.
