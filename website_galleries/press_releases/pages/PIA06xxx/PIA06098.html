# Wide View of Saturn's F Ring * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06098_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06098.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (25.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06098.tif) (1024 x 1024) (311.1 kB)

Caption:

This is one of the first images taken of Saturn's F ring by the Cassini spacecraft after it successfully entered Saturn's orbit. It was taken by the spacecraft's wide angle camera and shows the sunlit side of the rings.
