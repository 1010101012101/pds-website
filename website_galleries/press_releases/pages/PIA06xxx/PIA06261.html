# Preparing to Dip * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06261_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06261.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (70.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06261.tif) (1024 x 1024) (824.4 kB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars Exploration Rover Opportunity looks back out at the plains of Meridiani Planum from the rover's first dip inside the rim of "Endurance Crater." Opportunity's rear hazard-avoidance camera took this picture during the rover's 133rd martian day, or sol, on June 8, 2004.
