# Bunge Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA06xxx/PIA06813_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA06813.jpg) (825 x 3036) (376.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA06813.tif) (825 x 3036) (2.5 MB)

Caption:

21 August 2004
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows low albedo (dark), windblown sand dunes on the floor of Bunge Crater, located near 33.8°S, 48.9°W. The image covers an area about 3 km (1.9 mi) across. Sunlight illuminates the scene from the upper left.
