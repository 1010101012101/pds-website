# Volcanic Flows * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20779_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20779.jpg) (1382 x 2643) (512.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20779.tif) (1382 x 2643) (2.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of the extensive lava flows of the Tharsis volcanic region. Note that the flows have encircled the two craters, covering the ejecta deposits, but not breaching/over running the crater rim.
