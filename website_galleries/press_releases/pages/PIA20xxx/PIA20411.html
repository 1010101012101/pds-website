# Arabia Terra * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20411_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20411.jpg) (1380 x 2642) (256.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20411.tif) (1380 x 2642) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a portion of Arabia Terra. The very narrow linear ridges are indications of tectonic processes at some point in the formation of this region.

Orbit Number: 62224 Latitude: 10.6429 Longitude: 1.18468 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-12-24 07:07
