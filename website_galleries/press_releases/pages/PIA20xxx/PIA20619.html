# Nilokeras Fossa * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20619_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20619.jpg) (1404 x 2642) (234.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20619.tif) (1404 x 2642) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows parallel sets of depressions. These are tectonic graben formed when a block of material is down dropped between paired faults. Nilokeras Fossa is located on the margin of Kasei Valles.

Orbit Number: 63224 Latitude: 24.5752 Longitude: 302.591 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-03-15 15:52
