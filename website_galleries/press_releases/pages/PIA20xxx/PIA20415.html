# Crater Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20415_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20415.jpg) (702 x 1434) (67.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20415.tif) (702 x 1434) (653.7 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small crater located on the floor of a larger crater. Sand dunes are visible on the floors of both craters. These unnamed craters are located in Arabia Terra.

Orbit Number: 62274 Latitude: 10.6023 Longitude: 358.441 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-12-28 09:57
