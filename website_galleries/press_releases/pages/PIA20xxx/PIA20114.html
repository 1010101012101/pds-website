# More Olympica Fossae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20114_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20114.jpg) (1398 x 2645) (392.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20114.tif) (1398 x 2645) (2.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a different portion of Olympica Fossae from yesterday's image. The east/west alignment is a very uniform width, in this region the fracturing widens as the feature makes a turn to the south.

Orbit Number: 61342 Latitude: 25.6155 Longitude: 247.135 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-10-12 15:56
