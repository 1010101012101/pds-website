# Olympia Undae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20623_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20623.jpg) (1181 x 2603) (424.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20623.tif) (1181 x 2603) (3.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of Olympia Undae, the largest of the many dune fields surrounding the north polar cap. This image was collected during summer, and the dunes are frost free.

Orbit Number: 63349 Latitude: 80.8968 Longitude: 129.99 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-03-25 23:22
