# South Polar Texture * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20974_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20974.jpg) (976 x 2623) (482.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20974.tif) (976 x 2623) (2.6 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image of the south polar cap shows a surface with hundreds of circular depressions. This texture similar in appearance to swiss cheese.

Orbit Number: 64535 Latitude: -86.8715 Longitude: 354.786 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-07-01 14:50
