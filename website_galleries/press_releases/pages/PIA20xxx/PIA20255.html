# Ridges * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20255_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20255.jpg) (707 x 1444) (70.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20255.tif) (707 x 1444) (702.5 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of the boundary region between Terra Sabaea and Utopia Planitia. The ridges and valleys in this image were likely created by tectonic faulting. There is a small channel entering from the right side of the image. Material from this channel has been deposited in a circular region, which may be an impact crater, and then down one of the valleys.
