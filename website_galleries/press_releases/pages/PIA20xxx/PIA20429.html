# Tyrrhena Terra - False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20429_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20429.jpg) (791 x 1584) (133.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20429.tif) (791 x 1584) (2.6 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The THEMIS camera contains 5 filters. The data from different filters can be combined in multiple ways to create a false color image. These false color images may reveal subtle variations of the surface not easily identified in a single band image. Today's false color image shows part of Tyrrhena Terra. The top of the image is in Savich Crater and the low region at the bottom of the image is Peraea Cavus.
