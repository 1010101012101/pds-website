# NASA to Launch Mars Rover in 2020 (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20760_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20760.jpg) (3000 x 2250) (528.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20760.tif) (3000 x 2250) (13.5 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars 2020 Project will re-use the basic engineering of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory/Curiosity to send a different rover to Mars, with new objectives and instruments. This artist's concept depicts the top of the 2020 rover's mast.

Background Info:

NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, a division of the California Institute of Technology, Pasadena, manages NASA's Mars Exploration Program for the NASA Science Mission Directorate, Washington.
