# Abalos Undae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20606_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20606.jpg) (1177 x 2597) (165.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20606.tif) (1177 x 2597) (2.3 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows the north polar scarp, with the polar cap in the upper left corner. Trending from the lower left corner to the center of the image are the dark dunes of Abalos Undae. This image is slightly west of Monday's image.
