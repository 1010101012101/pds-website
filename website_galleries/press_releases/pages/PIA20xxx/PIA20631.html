# Hebrus Valles * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20631_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20631.jpg) (1399 x 2644) (404.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20631.tif) (1399 x 2644) (2.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a portion of Hebrus Valles. This channel system is located west of the Elysium volcanic complex. The streamlined islands in the channel indicate the fluid flow was towards the upper left of the image.

Orbit Number: 63542 Latitude: 19.654 Longitude: 126.493 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-04-10 20:26
