# Olympica Fossae * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20222_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20222.jpg) (1399 x 2645) (385.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20222.tif) (1399 x 2645) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a portion of Olympica Fossae. In this image several lava channels are visible, and it appears that lava has flowed in the larger depressions. The streamlined feature in the central part of the image also indicates that volcanic processes were active in this region.
