Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of Daedalia Planum. The volcanic flows of Daedalia Planum originated from Arsia Mons.

Orbit Number: 63626 Latitude: -7.39667 Longitude: 226.062 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-04-17 18:20
