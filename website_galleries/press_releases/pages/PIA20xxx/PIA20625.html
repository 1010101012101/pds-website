Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of Mamers Valles. Mamers Valles is a large complex set of channels in northern Arabia Terra that flow into Acidalia Planitia.

Orbit Number: 63421 Latitude: 35.6929 Longitude: 15.9775 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-03-31 21:20
