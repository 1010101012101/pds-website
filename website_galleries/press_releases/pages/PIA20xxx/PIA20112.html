# Tenuis Cavus * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20112_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20112.jpg) (1175 x 2585) (212.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20112.tif) (1175 x 2585) (2.3 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Tenuis Cavus is a region within the north polar cap where the underlying surface is visible. Ice comprises the top part of the image. Craters and sand dunes are visible on the ice-free surface.

Orbit Number: 61283 Latitude: 84.7719 Longitude: 2.80914 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-10-07 19:42
