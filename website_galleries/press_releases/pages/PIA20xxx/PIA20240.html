# Concentric Rims * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20240_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20240.jpg) (1407 x 2651) (375.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20240.tif) (1407 x 2651) (2.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows an unnamed crater near Acheron Fossae. The structure of this crater includes a central pit and several concentric rims. Radial grooves can be seen on the ejecta blanket surrounding the crater.

Orbit Number: 61879 Latitude: 36.8829 Longitude: 232.325 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-11-25 21:21
