# Polar Textures * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20993_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20993.jpg) (969 x 2627) (578.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20993.tif) (969 x 2627) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of the South Polar cap. In this region the surface has numerous circular depressions, which give the appearance of a slice of swiss cheese.

Orbit Number: 64848 Latitude: -86.7725 Longitude: 355.096 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-07-27 09:35
