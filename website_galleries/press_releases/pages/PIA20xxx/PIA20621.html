# Huo Hsing Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20621_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20621.jpg) (711 x 1445) (87.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20621.tif) (711 x 1445) (740.0 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of Huo Hsing Vallis, located on the northern margin of Terra Sabaea. Numerous channels are located in this region, draining the highland as the elevation drops down into Utopia Planitia.

Orbit Number: 63307 Latitude: 30.3507 Longitude: 66.5621 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-03-22 11:58
