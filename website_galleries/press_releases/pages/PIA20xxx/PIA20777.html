# Hydraotes Chaos * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20777_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20777.jpg) (696 x 1432) (63.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20777.tif) (696 x 1432) (685.9 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of Hydraotes Chaos. Chaos is defined as a distinctive area of broken terrain. Topographically, chaos regions have hills/mesas/plateaus surroundied by lower elevation valleys that crisscross in random directions.

Orbit Number: 63872 Latitude: 1.633 Longitude: 325.687 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2016-05-08 00:41
