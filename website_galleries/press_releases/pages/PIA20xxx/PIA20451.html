# Terra Sabaea Channels * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA20xxx/PIA20451_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA20451.jpg) (709 x 1445) (71.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA20451.tif) (709 x 1445) (710.0 kB)

Caption:


Context image

The northern margin of Terra Sabaea has numerous channels. This VIS image shows a portion of an unnamed channel on the margin of Terra Sabaea.
