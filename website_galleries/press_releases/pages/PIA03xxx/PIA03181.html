# Stardust Returns to Earth (Artist's Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03181_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03181.jpg) (1280 x 720) (67.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03181.tif) (1280 x 720) (2.8 MB)

Caption:

This artist's rendering depicts Stardust returning to Earth. Stardust is the first U.S. space mission dedicated solely to the exploration of a comet, and the first robotic mission designed to return extraterrestrial material from outside the orbit of the Moon. The Stardust spacecraft will bring back samples of interstellar dust, including recently discovered dust streaming into our Solar System from the direction of Sagittarius.
