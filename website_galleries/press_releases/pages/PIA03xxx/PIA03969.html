# South Amazonis Yardangs * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03969_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03969.jpg) (2048 x 4050) (1.1 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03969.tif) (2048 x 4050) (8.3 MB)

Caption:

27 June 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows classic yardangs , landforms created by wind erosion in southern Amazonis Planitia. The boat-shaped mesa, in particular, is a classic example of a yardang landform. Yardangs are found on both Earth and Mars.

Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
