# East Candor Outcrops * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03922_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03922.jpg) (2048 x 2860) (646.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03922.tif) (2048 x 2860) (5.9 MB)

Caption:

30 May 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows light-toned, wind-eroded, sedimentary rock outcrops in eastern Candor Chasma, part of the Valles Marineris trough system.

Location near : 7.7°S, 65.3°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Southern Spring
