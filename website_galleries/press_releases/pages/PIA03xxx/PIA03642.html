# Dust Devil Art * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03642_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03642.jpg) (1024 x 2128) (592.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03642.tif) (1024 x 2128) (2.2 MB)

Caption:

12 December 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows dark squiggles and streaks created by passing spring and summer dust devils near Pallacopas Vallis in the martian southern hemisphere.

Location near : 53.9°S, 17.2°W

Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
