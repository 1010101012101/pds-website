# Sample Return Capsule * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03669_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03669.jpg) (3008 x 2000) (556.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03669.tif) (3008 x 2000) (18.1 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Stardust sample return capsule successfully landed at the U.S. Air Force Utah Test and Training Range at 2:10 a.m. Pacific time (3:10 a.m. Mountain time). The capsule contains cometary and interstellar samples gathered by the Stardust spacecraft.
