# Copernicus Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03258_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03258.jpg) (512 x 851) (106.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03258.tif) (512 x 851) (436.4 kB)

Caption:

22 December 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows dark teardrop-shaped sand dunes in eastern Copernicus Crater. The winds responsible for these dunes generally blow from the south-southwest (lower left).

Location near : 48.7°S, 167.4°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
