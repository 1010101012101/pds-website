# Next Vital Step: Spacecraft Delivery * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03523_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03523.jpg) (720 x 478) (59.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03523.tif) (720 x 478) (1.0 MB)

Caption:

The Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter was delivered in two large containers from Lockheed Martin to Cape Canaveral on an Air Force C-17 cargo plane. Over the next several months, engineers and technicians will prepare the spacecraft for its scheduled launch in August.
