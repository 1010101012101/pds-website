# Polar Layers * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03278_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03278.jpg) (672 x 1056) (207.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03278.tif) (672 x 1056) (710.6 kB)

Caption:

6 January 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows layered material exposed on a slope in the south polar region of Mars. The composition of the layers, and whether they contain ice, is not known.

Location near : 87.0°S, 176.1°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
