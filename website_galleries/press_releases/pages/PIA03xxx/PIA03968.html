# West Tithonium Scene * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03968_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03968.jpg) (2048 x 2816) (794.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03968.tif) (2048 x 2816) (5.8 MB)

Caption:

26 June 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows dark rippled surfaces and a patch of light-toned, perhaps sedimentary rock exposed on the floor of western Tithonium Chasma, part of the vast Valles Marineris trough system.

Location near : 5.0°S, 90.3°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season Southern Spring
