# Chain of Pits * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03930_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03930.jpg) (836 x 1041) (68.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03930.tif) (836 x 1041) (871.4 kB)

Caption:

8 June 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a chain of pits associated with dust-covered lava flows in northern Tharsis. The pits formed along a fault; some of the flows may have erupted along this same fault.

Location near : 23.4°N, 110.6°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Autumn
