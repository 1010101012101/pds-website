# Gullied Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03619_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03619.jpg) (672 x 1764) (278.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03619.tif) (672 x 1764) (1.2 MB)

Caption:

5 December 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows gullies formed in the walls of an impact crater. Such gullies might result from seepage and runoff of groundwater; others have suggested snowmelt might be involved, although there is no evidence for snow at this location.
