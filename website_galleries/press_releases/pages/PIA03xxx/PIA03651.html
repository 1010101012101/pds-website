# Double Impact * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03651_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03651.jpg) (1024 x 1896) (677.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03651.tif) (1024 x 1896) (1.9 MB)

Caption:

19 December 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows two craters that formed at the same time by a split meteoritic impactor. Long after they formed, these craters have been eroded, degraded, and other materials have been deposited on and within them.
