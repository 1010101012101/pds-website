# Isidis Planitia * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03087_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03087.jpg) (1024 x 1908) (341.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03087.tif) (1024 x 1908) (2.0 MB)

Caption:

3 November 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a cratered surface in Isidis Planitia, a martian lowland. Light-toned "squiggles" in this August 2005 image are large windblown ripples.
