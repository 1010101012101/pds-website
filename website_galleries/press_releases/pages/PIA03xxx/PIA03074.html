# Crater and Flows * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03074_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03074.jpg) (672 x 2432) (382.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03074.tif) (672 x 2432) (1.6 MB)

Caption:

29 October 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a crater surrounded by thin flows in southeastern Kasei Valles. The flows might have been lava or mud. The picture was acquired in August 2005.

Location near : 14.2°N, 75.1°W
