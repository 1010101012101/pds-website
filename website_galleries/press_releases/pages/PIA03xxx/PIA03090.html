# Sedimentary Rocks * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03090_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03090.jpg) (836 x 1470) (185.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03090.tif) (836 x 1470) (1.2 MB)

Caption:

6 November 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows outcrops of sedimentary rocks in a crater located just north of the Sinus Meridiani region. Perhaps the crater was once the site of a martian lake.
