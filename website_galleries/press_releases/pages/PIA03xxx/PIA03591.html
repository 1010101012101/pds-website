# Mid-latitude Gullies * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03591_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03591.jpg) (2048 x 2315) (939.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03591.tif) (2048 x 2315) (4.7 MB)

Caption:

25 November 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a suite of south mid-latitude gullies on a crater wall. Gullies such as these may have formed by runoff of liquid water.

Location near : 38.0°S, 167.2°W
Image width : width: ~3 km (~1.9 mi)

Season : Southern Summer
