# South Polar Scene * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03680_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03680.jpg) (672 x 1296) (119.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03680.tif) (672 x 1296) (872.0 kB)

Caption:

19 January 2006
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a mid-summer scene in the south polar region of the red planet. The light-toned surface is covered with seasonal frost that, later in the season, would have sublimed away.

Location near : 86.8°S, 322.8°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : upper left
Season : Southern Summer
