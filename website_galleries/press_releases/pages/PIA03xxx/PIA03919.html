# Young Impact * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03919_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03919.jpg) (2048 x 2207) (685.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03919.tif) (2048 x 2207) (4.5 MB)

Caption:

27 May 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a small, relatively young impact crater in the Xanthe Terra region of Mars. Boulders can be seen in the crater ejecta deposit.

Location near : 2.3°N, 57.8°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Autumn
