# Olympica Fossae Landforms * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03951_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03951.jpg) (1254 x 1969) (317.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03951.tif) (1254 x 1969) (2.5 MB)

Caption:

15 June 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows a portion of the enigmatic valley of the Olympica Fossae region. Unknown is whether water, lava, or mud, or some combination of these things, once poured through the valley system.

Location near : 24.2°N, 115.7°W
