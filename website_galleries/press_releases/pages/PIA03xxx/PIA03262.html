# Buried Craters * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03262_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03262.jpg) (512 x 1048) (204.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03262.tif) (512 x 1048) (537.3 kB)

Caption:

26 December 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows two circular features on the plains of northern Utopia. A common sight on the martian northern plains, these rings indicate the locations of buried impact craters.

Location near : 65.1°N, 261.2°W
Image width : ~2 km (~1.2 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season : Northern Summer
