# 'Algonquin' Outcrop on Spirit's Sol 680 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03614_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03614.jpg) (3712 x 1120) (831.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03614.tif) (3712 x 1120) (4.2 MB)

Caption:

This view combines four frames from Spirit's panoramic camera, looking in the drive direction on the rover's 680th Martian day, or sol (Dec. 1, 2005). The outcrop of apparently layered bedrock has the informal name "Algonquin."
