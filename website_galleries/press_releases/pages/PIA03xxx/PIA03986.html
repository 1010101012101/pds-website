# Nili Patera Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA03xxx/PIA03986_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA03986.jpg) (2048 x 4992) (1.4 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA03986.tif) (2048 x 4992) (10.2 MB)

Caption:

6 July 2005
This Mars Global Surveyor (MGS) Mars Orbiter Camera (MOC) image shows dark, windblown sand dunes in the caldera of Nili Patera, a volcanic crater in Syrtis Major. The dunes were formed by winds blowing from the northeast (upper right).

Location near : 9.0°N, 292.9°W
Image width : ~3 km (~1.9 mi)
Illumination from : lower left
Season Northern Autumn
