# Rare Glance at Dark Side of Comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19041_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19041.jpg) (2048 x 2048) (778.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19041.tif) (2048 x 2048) (4.2 MB)

Caption:

This is a rare glance at the dark side of comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko. Light backscattered from dust particles in the comet's coma reveals a hint of surface structures. This image was taken by OSIRIS, Rosetta's scientific imaging system, on Sept. 29, 2014 from a distance of approximately 11.8 miles (19 kilometers).
