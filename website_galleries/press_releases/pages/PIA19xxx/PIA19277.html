# Daedalia Planum * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19277_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19277.jpg) (677 x 1418) (162.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19277.tif) (677 x 1418) (961.2 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a small portion of the lava flows that make up Daedalia Planum. These flows originate at Arsia Mons.

Orbit Number: 58379 Latitude: -24.4283 Longitude: 237.799 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-02-10 15:13
