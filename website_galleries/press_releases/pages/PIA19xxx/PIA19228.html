# Russel Crater Dunes - IR * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19228_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19228.jpg) (320 x 1808) (86.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19228.tif) (320 x 1808) (579.4 kB)

Caption:


Context image

In this infrared image of the dunes on the floor of Russell Crater, the dunes are brighter than the surround materials. This shows that the dunes are warmer than the nearby rocky material.

Latitude: -54.4765 Longitude: 13.0926 Instrument: IR Captured: 2015-01-21 17:09
