# Dawn Survey Orbit Image 36 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19609_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19609.jpg) (1024 x 1024) (187.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19609.tif) (1024 x 1024) (1.1 MB)

Caption:

This image, taken by NASA's Dawn spacecraft, shows Dantu crater on dwarf planet Ceres from an altitude of 2,700 miles (4,400 kilometers). The image, with a resolution of 1,400 feet (410 meters) per pixel, was taken on June 24, 2015. Dantu, at bottom center, is about 75 miles (120 kilometers) across and 3 miles (5 kilometers) deep, has small patches of bright material sprinkled around it.
