# NASA's Asteroid Redirect Robotic Mission (Artist Concept) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19349_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19349.jpg) (1226 x 689) (62.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19349.tif) (1226 x 689) (1.4 MB)

Caption:

Artists concept of NASA's Asteroid Redirect Robotic (ARM) Mission capturing an asteroid boulder before redirecting it to a astronaut-accessible orbit around Earth's moon.

More information about NASA's Asteroid Redirect Mission and the agency's Asteroid Initiative is available online at http://www.nasa.gov/mission_pages/asteroids/initiative/index.html .
