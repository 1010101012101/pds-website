# Gully and Fan-Shaped Deposit on Vesta * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19170_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19170.jpg) (6405 x 3877) (1.8 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19170.tif) (6405 x 3877) (24.9 MB)

Caption:

This image shows Cornelia Crater on the large asteroid Vesta. The crater is about 4 to 5 million years old. On the right is an inset image showing an example of curved gullies, indicated by the short white arrows, and a fan-shaped deposit, indicated by long white arrows. The inset image is about 0.62 miles (1 kilometer) wide.
