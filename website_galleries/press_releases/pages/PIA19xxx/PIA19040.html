# Dark Side of Comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19040_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19040.jpg) (2048 x 2048) (458.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19040.tif) (2048 x 2048) (4.2 MB)

Caption:

This image of comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko was obtained on October 30, 2014 by the OSIRIS scientific imaging system on the Rosetta spacecraft. The right half is obscured by darkness. The image was taken from a distance of approximately 18.6 miles (30 kilometers)
