# Central Pit Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19476_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19476.jpg) (1336 x 2654) (269.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19476.tif) (1336 x 2654) (3.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows an unnamed crater in Noachis Terra. This crater has a pit in the center of the floor of the crater. Craters can have several different features at this location, including a peak, a pit, or a smooth floor.

Orbit Number: 59099 Latitude: -27.2598 Longitude: 347.826 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-04-10 22:06
