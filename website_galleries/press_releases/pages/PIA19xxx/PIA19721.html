# Cavi Angusti * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA19xxx/PIA19721_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA19721.jpg) (504 x 1410) (59.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA19721.tif) (504 x 1410) (711.6 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of Cavi Angusti, a region of depressions near the south polar cap. The linear ridges in the image were likely formed by tectonic activity.

Orbit Number: 59487 Latitude: -81.0714 Longitude: 291.416 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-05-12 21:11
