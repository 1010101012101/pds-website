Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image was collected at the same time as yesterday's IR image. The dunes are dark in the VIS image, because the material is darker in color than the surrounding material.

Latitude: -54.3016 Longitude: 13.1403 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2015-01-21 17:09
