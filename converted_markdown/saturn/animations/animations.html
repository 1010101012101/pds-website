# Saturn's Ring System: Animations [The Voyager 2 flyby of Saturn](/www/saturn/animations/SATURN_FLY-BY.QT) (Quicktime format, 822 Kbytes) This animation illustrates the geometry of Voyager 2's passage through the Saturn system. This was a NASA full motion video clip, converted to QuickTime format. [A "spoke" movie](/www/saturn/animations/saturn_spoke.mov) (Quicktime format, 1.02 Mbytes) A sequence of Voyager images was used to generate this movie showing the motion of the mysterious "spokes" in Saturn's B Ring. [A stellar occulation by Saturn's rings](/www/saturn/animations/saturn_pps_v.mov) (Quicktime format, 1.02 Mbytes) The Voyager 2 [ photopolarimeter (PPS)](/www/voyager/hardware/pps/pps.html) and [ ultraviolet spectrometer (UVS)](/www/voyager/hardware/uvs/uvs.html) instruments observed the occultation of the star delta Sco by Saturn's rings. These experiments resolved the rings down to a scale of 100 meters.
