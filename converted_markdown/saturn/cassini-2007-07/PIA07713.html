Scientists are trying to determine whether these features are solid moonlets that help control the ring or just loose clumps of particles within the ring. The images in this sequence were acquired in visible light using the Cassini spacecraft narrow-angle camera on April 13, 2005, at a distance of approximately 1.2 million kilometers (700,000 miles) from Saturn.
