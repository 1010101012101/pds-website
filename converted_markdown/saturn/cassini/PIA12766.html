See [PIA12710](http://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA12710) and [PIA12785](http://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA12785) to learn more about the F ring and its unusual "fan" features. This view looks toward the southern, unilluminated side of the rings from about 1 degree below the ringplane. The image was taken in visible light with the Cassini spacecraft narrow-angle camera on Oct. 16, 2010. The view was acquired at a distance of approximately 320,000 kilometers (199,000 miles) from the F ring. Image scale is 2 kilometers (1 mile) per pixel.
