# Saturn's F Ring ![Saturn's F Ring](./PIA02292-med.jpg) [ Large size JPG image (54670 bytes)](./PIA02292.jpg) **Caption: ** This narrow-angle camera image of Saturn's F Ring was taken through the Clear filter while at a distance of 0.75 million km from Saturn on 12 November 1980. The kinks and braids of this tightly-constrained ring are visible along with the outer edge of the A Ring. JPL managed the Voyager Project for NASA's Office of Space Science.
