Profiles of both the lit and dark sides are included at various resolutions of order 5 km. We generally recommend using the data files sampled at 2-km resolution: * Dark side mosaic: [ IS1_P0001_V01_KM002.TAB](/volumes/VG_28xx/VG_2810/DATA/IS1_P0001_V01_KM002.TAB) and [.LBL](/volumes/VG_28xx/VG_2810/DATA/IS1_P0001_V01_KM002.LBL) * Lit side mosaic: [ IS2_P0001_V01_KM002.TAB](/volumes/VG_28xx/VG_2810/DATA/IS2_P0001_V01_KM002.TAB) and [.LBL](/volumes/VG_28xx/VG_2810/DATA/IS2_P0001_V01_KM002.LBL) * Other resolutions are available in the same directory, [DATA](/vol/VG_28xx/VG_2810/DATA/). You can also download the entire volume as a tar.gz file [here](/archives/VG_28xx/VG_2810.tar.gz).
